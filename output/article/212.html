Announcement on the Eastern Theater Command's 2026 Open Recruitment of Professionally Skilled Civilian Personnel from Society

东部战区2026年面向社会公开招考专业技能类文职人员公告
PLA Daily (解放军报) 13 May 2026
View original source ↗
About this analysis
Summary
The Eastern Theater Command's Political Work Department published its 2026 open recruitment announcement for professionally skilled civilian personnel, soliciting applicants from society for support-role positions—cook, driver, storekeeper, cafeteria manager—at Professional Skills Grade 3 and below, with an online registration window of May 18–24, 2026. The announcement follows a standard annual pattern for PLA civilian personnel recruitment under the Regulations on Civilian Personnel of the People's Liberation Army and contains no new information about force structure, capability, or posture; its primary analytic utility is as a baseline data point on Eastern Theater Command's routine civilian workforce management and the preference system it maintains for military families and veterans.
Translation

In accordance with the Regulations on Civilian Personnel of the People's Liberation Army and relevant provisions, the following announcement is hereby made regarding matters pertaining to the Eastern Theater Command's 2026 open recruitment of professionally skilled civilian personnel from society:

I. Recruitment Positions

Technical worker positions at or below Professional Skills Grade 3 in relevant units of the Eastern Theater Command, including cook, driver, storekeeper, cafeteria manager, and similar positions. See the recruitment position plan for details.

II. Recruitment Targets

Members of society who meet the qualification requirements for the positions. The following persons are not permitted to apply:

(1) Persons who have received criminal punishment for a crime;

(2) Persons who have been expelled from the Chinese Communist Party;

(3) Persons who have been dismissed from public office;

(4) Persons who have been lawfully designated as subjects of joint disciplinary action for breach of trust;

(5) Persons who have been found to have committed fraud or other serious violations of recruitment discipline in civil servant, public institution staff, or military civilian personnel recruitment examinations;

(6) Persons who, within the past 3 years, voluntarily relinquished their eligibility for appointment after being publicly announced as a proposed appointee for a military civilian personnel position;

(7) Persons who have been dismissed (unilaterally had their employment contract terminated) by a military employing unit or a government or public institution unit and for whom fewer than 5 years have elapsed;

(8) Active-duty military personnel and currently serving military civilian personnel (as of the date of this announcement);

(9) In principle, directionally enrolled students (定向生) and commission-trained students (委培生) graduating in 2026 (as of the date of this announcement) are not permitted to apply;

(10) Persons whom laws and regulations prohibit from being recruited or employed as civilian personnel.

Social talent who have already established a labor or personnel relationship with a state, local, or military employing unit may apply after obtaining consent from their current employing unit. At the time of registration, the applicant must provide a written consent-to-apply certificate issued by their current employing unit (bearing the official seal of the organization or human resources department). Those designated as proposed appointees must terminate their labor or personnel relationship with their original employing unit before establishing a personnel relationship with the military employing unit.

In addition, applicants may not apply for positions in employing units whose leadership includes military personnel with whom the applicant has a spousal relationship, direct lineal blood relationship, collateral blood relationship within three generations, or close affinal relationship. Applicants also may not apply for positions in which both the applicant and a military person with whom they share a spousal relationship, direct lineal blood relationship, or collateral blood relationship within three generations are directly subordinate to the same superior within the same employing unit, or for positions involving a direct superior-subordinate relationship, nor for sensitive positions such as human resources, discipline inspection and supervision, auditing, finance, or military facilities construction in which military personnel hold leadership roles.

III. Application Requirements

Applicants must meet the basic conditions stipulated in the Regulations on Civilian Personnel of the People's Liberation Army and additionally satisfy the following qualification requirements:

(I) Political conditions. Must comply with relevant regulations governing political review for the recruitment and employment of military civilian personnel.

(II) Qualification conditions. Must have obtained the vocational qualification or professional (trade) certification required by the recruitment position, or possess the capabilities required by the position, as specified by the relevant certificates required under the "Other Conditions" section of the position listing.

(III) Educational conditions. Must hold an associate degree (大学专科) or higher. Where a recruitment position requires full-time study, the applicant must meet the educational level and major requirements corresponding to the position. Applicants with higher educational qualifications may apply for positions with lower educational requirements, but must hold a major corresponding to the educational level required by the recruitment position.

(IV) Age conditions. For Professional Skills Grade 3 and Grade 4 positions: no younger than 18 years of age and no older than 35 years of age (born between May 1990 and May 2008). For Professional Skills Grade 5 positions: no younger than 18 years of age and no older than 30 years of age (born between May 1995 and May 2008).

(V) Physical conditions. Must comply with relevant regulations governing physical examinations for the recruitment and employment of military civilian personnel.

(VI) Other conditions. Must meet any other qualification requirements specified for the position.

Notes: ① Where "XX or above" is referenced, the stated level, year threshold, etc., are inclusive. ② The deadline for applicants to have obtained their educational credentials is July 31, 2026; the deadline for obtaining vocational qualification certificates and vocational skills level certificates is May 24, 2026.

IV. Recruitment Procedures

(I) Information release. The Eastern Theater Command will publicly release the recruitment announcement and position plan to society through the Military Talent Network (website: http://www.81rc.mil.cn or http://81rc.81.cn). Applicants who have questions regarding the major, educational, vocational skills, work experience, or other qualification requirements for a position may call the position inquiry telephone number.

(II) Online registration. The registration period runs from 08:00 on May 18, 2026 to 18:00 on May 24, 2026. Applicants must log in to the "2026 Military Open Recruitment of Professionally Skilled Civilian Personnel Registration System" on the Military Talent Network (registration portal) to register, truthfully complete personal information in accordance with the procedural requirements, and carefully select the employing unit and recruitment position. When selecting a position, applicants must verify item by item whether they meet the corresponding application requirements. No in-person registration will be available for this recruitment.

Applicants must upload supporting documents corresponding to the qualification requirements of the position for which they are applying, including identity card, household registration booklet (户口本), diploma, driver's license, vocational qualification (skills level) certificate, proof of employment, labor and personnel contract, certificate of discharge from active service, and other supporting materials (supporting materials must be in JPG format; where multiple documents exist for a single condition, they must be merged into one file; file size must be controlled between 100 KB and 1 MB; images must be clear and legible).

Applicants who fall into the following categories must truthfully complete the identity category in the "Military Martyrs' Dependents and Similar Personnel (军烈属等人员)" field of the registration system; such persons will receive bonus points on the theory examination. Applicants who fail to complete the corresponding identity category at the time of registration will be deemed to have voluntarily waived their eligibility for preferential bonus points. ① Spouses and children of military martyrs and military personnel who died in the line of duty, and siblings of unmarried military martyrs. ② Spouses and children of active-duty military personnel, military personnel who retired in the current year (with a retirement order date between April 1, 2025 and April 1, 2026), military personnel who died of illness while on active duty, and active-duty officers who have converted to civilian personnel status; social contract workers (社会用工) who have worked in military units for a cumulative period of 5 or more years (as of May 2026); and veterans who have served for 5 or more years. ③ Social contract workers and veterans who participated in combat operations, military operations with a combat background, or non-war military operations approved by the Central Military Commission or departments of the Central Military Commission.

No registration fee will be charged for the 2026 Eastern Theater Command professionally skilled civilian personnel recruitment. Applicants must accurately provide their personal contact information when completing their registration, must maintain open communications throughout the entire registration period, and must promptly monitor recruitment information published on the Military Talent Network to avoid missing application opportunities.

(III) Initial qualification review. From 08:00 on May 18, 2026 to 18:00 on May 26, 2026, employing units will conduct an initial qualification review of the information submitted by applicants through the Military Talent Network registration system to confirm whether applicants meet the application requirements; results of the initial review will be returned within 48 hours of registration. Applicants who pass the initial qualification review may not modify their registration information or switch to a different position. Applicants who do not pass the initial qualification review within the registration period may switch to a different position. Applicants who do not pass the initial qualification review during the period from 18:00 on May 24 to 18:00 on May 25 may, before 18:00 on May 26, revise and supplement the relevant supporting materials as required and resubmit for review, but may not switch to a different position. Applicants who do not pass the initial qualification review will not be scheduled to participate in the examination and assessment. Qualification review applies throughout the entire recruitment process; where an applicant is found at any stage of the recruitment process to not meet the application qualification requirements, their examination and assessment scores and eligibility for appointment will be cancelled.

(IV) Theory examination. Employing units will notify applicants of the time and location of the theory examination by telephone, text message, email, or other means (applicants must maintain open communications). Applicants must bring a valid resident identity card or other valid identification to the theory examination as notified by the employing unit. After the theory examination, the list of qualifying candidates will be determined in descending order of theory examination scores (including preferential bonus points) in accordance with the qualifying ratio specified in the position plan.

The following persons who have passed the initial registration qualification review and achieved a valid theory examination score will receive preferential bonus points on their examination scores: ① Spouses and children of military martyrs and military personnel who died in the line of duty, and siblings of unmarried military martyrs: 10 points added to the theory examination score. ② Spouses and children of active-duty military personnel, military personnel who retired in the current year, military personnel who died of illness while on active duty, and active-duty officers who have converted to civilian personnel status; social contract workers who have worked in military units for a cumulative period of 5 or more years; and veterans who have served for 5 or more years: 5 points added to the theory examination score. ③ Social contract workers and veterans who participated in combat operations, military operations with a combat background, or non-war military operations approved by the Central Military Commission or departments of the Central Military Commission, and who have been assessed as outstanding by the dispatching employing unit: 5 points added to the theory examination score. Among these, those who are also spouses or children of military martyrs or military personnel who died in the line of duty, or siblings of unmarried military martyrs, will have 15 points added to their theory examination score; those who are also spouses or children of active-duty military personnel, military personnel who retired in the current year, military personnel who died of illness while on active duty, or active-duty officers who have converted to civilian personnel status, will have 10 points added to their theory examination score. Where an applicant simultaneously qualifies for bonus points under multiple provisions above, the bonus points are not cumulative; the highest applicable bonus shall apply.

(V) Qualification re-verification. Employing units will re-verify the original supporting documents for qualifying candidates' identity information, educational credentials and major, vocational qualifications, work experience, and other materials. Those whose results are satisfactory will proceed to the skills assessment.

(VI) Skills assessment. Around late June, the skills assessment will be organized in conjunction with relevant military and civilian professional institutions, using a combination of on-site oral examination and practical operation. The specific time and relevant requirements will be communicated to qualifying candidates by the employing unit via email, telephone, or other means.

(VII) Physical examination. For candidates who achieve a passing score of 60 or above on the skills assessment, the employing unit will organize a physical examination in accordance with relevant military regulations. The physical examination will be conducted at a designated medical institution, with costs borne by the military.

(VIII) Determination of pre-selected candidates. The full score for both the theory examination and the skills assessment is 100 points each. The composite score is calculated on a 100-point scale, with the theory examination and skills assessment scores accounting for 40% and 60% respectively; scores are retained to two decimal places. Employing units will determine the list of pre-selected candidates in descending order of composite score at 120% of the planned recruitment number. Candidates whose skills assessment score is below 60 or who fail the physical examination may not be designated as pre-selected candidates. Where composite scores are equal, ranking will be determined successively by skills assessment score and then theory examination score.

(IX) Political review. Relevant military and civilian units will conduct a political review of pre-selected candidates in accordance with relevant regulations governing political review of military conscription and recruitment personnel. The political review focuses primarily on the individual's political and ideological conduct, with emphasis on: support for and implementation of the Party's theories, line, principles, and policies; compliance with state laws and regulations; political stance, political attitude, and actual conduct on major matters of principle and major political struggles; adherence to political discipline and political rules (政治纪律和政治规矩); moral character, religious beliefs, social associations, honesty and trustworthiness, and overseas travel (出国（境）); as well as the political background and criminal record of family members, the spouse's parents, and siblings not living together.

(X) Determination of proposed appointees. For pre-selected candidates whose political review conclusion is a pass, the employing unit will determine the list of proposed appointees in accordance with the number of positions in the recruitment plan, ranked by composite examination and assessment score, and will publicly announce the list through the Military Talent Network. For candidates about whom no objections are raised during the public announcement period, or where issues raised do not affect appointment, approval for appointment will be granted in accordance with the applicable authority, notification to report for probationary duty will be issued, and an employment contract will be signed.

V. Special Notice

No designated study guides are specified for the theory examination and skills assessment of the Eastern Theater Command's open recruitment of professionally skilled civilian personnel. No tutoring or training courses are organized or commissioned to any institution. All applicants are urged to remain vigilant, not to be misled or interfered with, and absolutely not to be deceived. The Military Talent Network (website: http://www.81rc.mil.cn or http://81rc.81.cn) is the sole designated website for information release and online registration for this open recruitment. Each employing unit has established an inquiry telephone number, which can be found in the position plan.

The final authority to interpret this recruitment process rests with the Eastern Theater Command Political Work Department. Recruitment inquiry telephone: 025-80907952.

根据《中国人民解放军文职人员条例》及有关规定，现将2026年东部战区专业技能类文职人员公开招考有关事项公告如下： 一、招考岗位 东部战区相关单位的炊事员、司机、保管员、食堂管理员等专业技能三级以下技术工岗位。详见招考岗位计划。 二、招考对象 符合岗位资格条件的社会人员。下列人员不得报考： （1）因犯罪受过刑事处罚的； （2）被开除中国共产党党籍的； （3）被开除公职的； （4）被依法列为失信联合惩戒对象的； （5）在公务员、事业单位工作人员和军队文职人员招考中被认定有舞弊等严重违反录用纪律行为的； （6）近3年作为军队文职人员拟录用对象公示后，本人自愿放弃录用资格的； （7）被军队用人单位或者机关事业单位辞退（单方面解除聘用合同）未满5年的； （8）现役军人、在职军队文职人员（公告发布之日）； （9）2026年毕业的定向生、委培生（公告发布之日），原则上不得报考； （10）法律、法规规定不得招录聘用为文职人员的。 已与国家、地方和军队用人单位建立劳动人事关系的社会人才，在征得所在用人单位同意后可以报考。报名时，本人需提供所在用人单位出具的同意报考证明（加盖组织或人事部门公章）；被确定为拟录用对象的，在与军队用人单位建立人事关系前，需与原用人单位解除劳动人事关系。 此外，报考人员不得报考与本人有夫妻关系、直系血亲关系、三代以内旁系血亲关系以及近姻亲关系的军队人员担任领导成员的用人单位的岗位，也不得报考与军队人员有夫妻关系、直系血亲关系、三代以内旁系血亲关系，在同一用人单位双方直接隶属于同一领导人员的岗位或者有直接上下级领导关系的岗位，以及军队人员担任领导职务的人力资源、纪检监察、审计、财务、军事设施建设等敏感岗位。 三、报考条件 报考人员应当符合《中国人民解放军文职人员条例》规定的基本条件，同时具备下列资格条件： （一）政治条件。符合军队招录聘用文职人员政治考核的有关规定。 （二）资格条件。取得招考岗位要求的相应专业（工种）职业资格或具备岗位要求能力，以岗位“其他条件”中要求的相关证书为准。 （三）学历条件。应当具有大学专科以上学历。招考岗位学历要求为全日制的，必须符合岗位相应层次学历及专业要求。高学历的可以报考低学历要求的岗位，但应当具有招考岗位要求学历对应的所学专业。 （四）年龄条件。专业技能三级、四级岗位，不小于18周岁、不超过35周岁（1990年5月至2008年5月期间出生）；专业技能五级岗位，不小于18周岁、不超过30周岁（1995年5月至2008年5月期间出生）。 （五）身体条件。应当符合军队招录聘用文职人员体格检查的相关规定。 （六）其他条件。应当符合岗位要求的其他资格条件。 其中：①涉及“XX以上”的，均包含本层级、本年限等；②报考人员取得学历截止时间为2026年7月31日，取得职业资格证书、职业技能等级证书截止时间为2026年5月24日。 四、招考程序 （一）发布信息。东部战区通过军队人才网（网址：http://www.81rc.mil.cn或者http://81rc.81.cn），面向社会公开发布招考公告和岗位计划。报考人员对岗位的专业、学历、职业技能、工作经历等资格条件有疑问的，可拨打岗位咨询电话。 （二）网上报名。报名时间为2026年5月18日8:00至5月24日18:00，报考人员登录军队人才网“2026年军队面向社会公开招考专业技能岗位文职人员报名系统”进行报名（报名入口），按照流程要求，如实填报个人信息，认真选择用人单位和招考岗位。选择岗位时，请务必逐条核对本人是否符合相应报考条件。此次招考不设现场报名。 报考人员需按照报考岗位资格条件要求，相应上传身份证、户口本、毕业证、驾驶证、职业资格（技能等级）证书、工作证明、人事劳动合同、退出现役证书等证明材料（证明材料为JPG文件格式，同一条件下有多项材料的，需合并为1个，文件大小控制在100KB至1MB之间，确保图片清晰可辨识）。 报考人员属下列人员的，需在报名系统“军烈属等人员”栏据实填报身份类别，理论考试享受加分优待；报名时未填报相应身份类别的，视为主动放弃优待加分资格。①军队烈士和因公牺牲军队人员的配偶子女、未婚军队烈士的兄弟姐妹。②现役军人、当年退役军人（退役命令时间为2025年4月1日至2026年4月1日）、在职病故军人和现役干部转改文职人员的配偶子女，在军队工作年限满5年的社会用工（截至2026年5月），以及服役年限满5年的退役军人。③参加由中央军委或者中央军委机关部委批准的作战、有作战背景的军事行动、非战争军事行动的社会用工和退役军人。 报考2026年东部战区专业技能类文职人员不收取报名费。报考人员填报个人信息时应当准确填写个人联系方式，整个报名期间须保持通讯畅通，并及时关注军队人才网发布的招考信息，以免错失报考机会。 （三）资格初审。2026年5月18日8:00至5月26日18:00，用人单位将通过军队人才网报名系统对报考人员填报的信息进行资格初审，确认是否符合报考条件，考生报名后48小时内反馈初审结果。资格初审通过的，不得修改报名信息或者改报其他岗位；报名时间内资格初审未通过的，可以改报其他岗位。5月24日18:00至5月25日18:00期间资格初审未通过的，可在5月26日18:00前，按要求修改完善相关证明材料后再次提交审核，但不能改报其他岗位。报考人员未通过资格初审的，不予安排参加考试考核。资格审查贯穿招考全程，在招考各环节发现报考人员不符合报考资格条件的，取消考试考核成绩及录用资格。 （四）理论考试。用人单位将通过电话、短信、邮件等方式通知报考人员理论考试时间地点（请保持通联方式畅通）。报考人员根据用人单位通知，携带居民身份证等有效证件参加理论考试。理论考试结束后，按照岗位计划入围比例，根据理论考试成绩（含优待加分）由高到低确定入围人员名单。 对通过报名资格初审且取得理论考试有效成绩的下列人员，考试成绩享受优待加分：①军队烈士和因公牺牲军队人员的配偶子女、未婚军队烈士的兄弟姐妹，理论考试成绩加10分。②现役军人、当年退役军人、在职病故军人和现役干部转改文职人员的配偶子女，在军队工作年限满5年的社会用工，以及服役年限满5年的退役军人，理论考试成绩加5分。③参加由中央军委或者中央军委机关部门批准的作战、有作战背景的军事行动、非战争军事行动的社会用工和退役军人，经派出用人单位鉴定表现突出的，理论考试成绩加5分。其中，属于军队烈士和因公牺牲军队人员的配偶子女、未婚军队烈士的兄弟姐妹的，理论考试成绩加15分；属于现役军人、当年退役军人、在职病故军人和现役干部转改文职人员的配偶子女的，理论考试成绩加10分。报考人员同时符合上述各项规定加分情形的，理论考试成绩加分分数不累计，按照最高情形确定。 （五）资格复核。用人单位对入围人员的身份信息、学历专业、职业资格、工作经历等证明材料原件进行复核，结果合格的参加技能考核。 （六）技能考核。6月下旬左右，依托军地相关专业机构，采用现场答辩和实际操作相结合的办法组织技能考核，具体时间和相关要求由用人单位通过电子邮件、电话等方式通知入围人员。 （七）体格检查。对技能考核成绩达到60分合格分数线的考生，用人单位按照军队有关规定组织体检。体检在指定的医疗机构进行，费用由军队承担。 （八）确定预选对象。理论考试、技能考核成绩满分均为100分，总成绩按照百分制折算，理论考试、技能考核成绩分别占40%、60%，成绩保留小数点后两位。用人单位按照计划招考人数120%的比例，根据总成绩由高到低确定预选对象。其中，技能考核成绩低于60分或者体检不合格的，不得作为预选对象；总成绩相同的，依次按照技能考核成绩、理论考试成绩确定排名。 （九）政治考核。军地有关单位按照军队征集和招录人员政治考核有关规定对预选对象进行政治考核。政治考核以考核本人政治思想表现为主，重点考核拥护执行党的理论和路线、方针、政策，遵守国家法律法规，重大原则问题、重大政治斗争中的政治立场、政治态度和现实表现，遵守政治纪律和政治规矩，道德品质、宗教信仰、社会交往、诚实守信和出国（境）等情况，以及家庭成员、配偶的父母、未共同生活的兄弟姐妹的政治背景和违法犯罪等情况。 （十）确定拟录用对象。对政治考核结论为通过的预选对象，用人单位根据考试考核总成绩排名，按照招考岗位计划人数确定拟录用对象名单，并通过军队人才网对外公示。对公示无异议或者反映问题不影响录用的人选，按权限审批录用，通知到岗试用，签订聘用合同。 五、特别提示 东部战区专业技能类文职人员公开招考理论考试和技能考核不指定辅导用书，不举办也不委托任何机构举办辅导培训班，敬请广大报考人员提高警惕，莫被误导干扰，切勿上当受骗。军队人才网（网址：http://www.81rc.mil.cn或者http://81rc.81.cn）为本次公开招考信息发布、网上报名等唯一指定网站。各用人单位设立咨询电话，可从岗位计划中查看。
