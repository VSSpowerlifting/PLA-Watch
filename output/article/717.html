Summary
The PAP Guizhou General Corps' Qianxinan Detachment conducted bayonet training recently, framing the exercise around cultivating 血性胆气 (fighting spirit) and 战斗精神 (combat spirit). This is standard close-combat conditioning content; its value is as a baseline record of how PAP internal units at the detachment level use physical training to reinforce ideological-martial identity, not as evidence of changed operational posture or readiness.
