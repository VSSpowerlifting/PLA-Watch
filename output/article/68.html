Summary
On July 23, 2020, Xi Jinping visited the Air Force Aviation University, observed flight cadets conducting rotating-ladder training, and personally encouraged them to train rigorously and contribute to PLA modernization under the 'strengthening the military' framework. The visit, produced by the PLA News and Communication Center's Current Affairs Department with involvement from Unit 95006, is a routine propaganda piece reinforcing Xi's role as commander-in-chief and the ideological framing of military education around his 'Thought on Strengthening the Military.' The announcement follows a standard pattern of leadership inspection coverage and contains no new operational or capability information.
Translation

On July 23, 2020, Chairman Xi inspected the Air Force Aviation University. Chairman Xi came to the training ground, where he saw flight cadets conducting rotating-ladder training, and warmly conversed with them. Cui Chong, as a representative of the flight cadets, reported his personal situation to Chairman Xi. Chairman Xi encouraged the flight cadets to study diligently, train hard, train scientifically, and become eagles of the blue sky as soon as possible, contributing their strength to building and strengthening the military (强军兴军).
