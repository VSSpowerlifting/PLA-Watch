Summary
The Central Military Commission issued a 26-article directive on the education, management, and supervision of senior military cadres, covering ideological remolding, Party committee collective leadership, intra-Party political life, cadre selection, and oversight of principal leaders. The directive extends the post-purge institutional response to the Rocket Force corruption scandal and the broader pattern of senior officer misconduct: it documents the CMC's attempt to formalize oversight mechanisms that failed to catch or deter the behavior that triggered the 2023–2024 leadership removals. The explicit emphasis on 'revolutionary tempering' and 'renewed political bearing' confirms that the CMC treats the senior cadre corps as requiring active rehabilitation, not merely tighter procedural controls.
The CMC's public issuance of a named, structured 26-article regulatory document specifically targeting senior military cadres—explicitly invoking ideological rectification (思想整风) and political consolidation training (政治整训)—signals a formal, institutionalized tightening of oversight over the senior officer corps, likely connected to the ongoing fallout from Rocket Force and broader PLA corruption investigations, and represents a documentable shift in the regulatory framework governing senior cadre conduct rather than routine political work messaging.

Central Military Commission Issues 'Several Measures on Strengthening the Education, Management, and Supervision of Senior Military Cadres'

PLA Daily, Beijing, May 27 — The Central Military Commission recently issued the 'Several Measures on Strengthening the Education, Management, and Supervision of Senior Military Cadres' (hereinafter referred to as the 'Several Measures').

The 'Several Measures' adhere to the guidance of Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era, thoroughly implement Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military (习近平强军思想), comprehensively carry out the strategy of building the military through politics in the new era (新时代政治建军方略), draw upon and apply the practical experience and institutional achievements of comprehensively and strictly governing the Party and comprehensively and strictly governing the military in the new era, implement the requirements for conducting ideological rectification (思想整风) and deepening political consolidation training (政治整训), and firmly establish iron rules (铁规矩) of strict education, strict management, and strict supervision.

The 'Several Measures' comprise 26 articles across 7 areas. With a focus on rigorously upholding discipline to alert the mind and guide conduct, strictly managing principal officers so that those above set an example for those below, and clearly defining responsibilities to ensure genuine and effective implementation, the measures establish rules and norms around: strengthening the consciousness and thoroughness of ideological remolding (思想改造); reinforcing collective Party committee leadership; enhancing the principled and combative character (原则性战斗性) of intra-Party political life; upholding the Party's management of cadres and organizational selection of personnel; and strengthening the supervision and management of principal leaders. The measures integrate strict and regular education, management, and supervision throughout the entire process of performing duties and exercising power, and across all aspects of work and daily life, in order to drive the revolutionary tempering (革命性锻造) of the senior cadre corps, so that with a renewed political bearing (崭新政治面貌) they may lead officers and soldiers in advancing the modernization of national defense and the military at high quality, and shoulder the historic responsibilities entrusted by the Party and the people.
