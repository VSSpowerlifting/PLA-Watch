PAP Chongqing Contingent Organizes Concentrated Showcase of 'Strengthening the Military Through Culture, Advancing Toward Combat'

武警重庆总队组织“文化强军向战行”集中展演
PLA Daily (解放军报) 28 May 2026
View original source ↗
About this analysis
Summary
The PAP Chongqing Contingent held a cultural showcase tied to training on the 'Outline for Grassroots Building in the Military' and Party organization secretary training, featuring performances by 11 grassroots cultural troupes alongside static exhibitions of soldier-produced art and a demonstration of the 'Five-Step Education Method.' This is standard political-cultural work content; its value is as a baseline record of how the PAP frames 'combat orientation' through cultural programming at the contingent level, and documents the institutional linkage between grassroots cultural development and formal political education cycles.
Translation

Recently, the People's Armed Police Chongqing Contingent organized a concentrated showcase of distinctive cultural programs in conjunction with training on the "Outline for Grassroots Building in the Military" (《军队基层建设纲要》) and Party organization secretary training.

According to an introduction, the showcase was themed "Strengthening the Military Through Culture, Advancing Toward Combat" (文化强军向战行), with an emphasis on a combat-oriented and soldier-oriented direction. It combined static displays with dynamic performances, and through graphic and textual presentations, audio-visual screenings, physical exhibitions, and live performances, comprehensively and systematically displayed the achievements of grassroots cultural development, with focused effort on enhancing the penetrating power (渗透力), infectious power (感染力), and transmissive power (传承力) of the force's cultural development.

During the dynamic performance segment, 11 distinctive cultural troupes from grassroots units took the stage in succession, delivering outstanding performances that drew repeated applause and cheers. After the viewing concluded, participants moved to the themed exhibition area to view original calligraphy and paintings, stone carvings and stone paintings, Qijiang woodblock prints, paper-cuts, clay sculptures, and other cultural and creative works produced by grassroots officers and soldiers, and exchanged on-site experiences in building distinctive unit cultures. At the venue, the contingent also organized a demonstration viewing of the "Five-Step Education Method" (教育五步法), further standardizing the order of education and instruction and clarifying teaching methods.

Original Chinese
近日，武警重庆总队结合《军队基层建设纲要》暨党组织书记培训，组织特色文化集中展演。 据介绍，此次展演以“文化强军向战行”为主题，突出战味、兵味导向，采取静态展示与动态展演相结合的方式，通过图文介绍、影音展播、实物展览、实地演出等全面系统展示基层文化建设成果，着力增强部队文化建设的渗透力、感染力与传承力。 动态展演环节，来自基层单位的11支特色文化队伍轮番上台，献上精彩表演，赢得阵阵掌声、喝彩声。观摩结束后，参训人员移步主题展区，参观基层官兵原创书法绘画、石刻石画、綦江版画、剪纸泥塑等文创作品，现地交流特色文化建设经验。现场，该总队还组织了“教育五步法”示范观摩，进一步正规抓教秩序、明确施教方法。
