Forge Strong and Powerful Grassroots Party Organizations

锻造坚强有力的基层党组织
PLA Daily (解放军报) 8 June 2026
View original source ↗
About this analysis
Summary
Two researchers at the People's Armed Police Force Research Institute published a doctrinal piece calling for strengthened grassroots Party organization building across the armed forces, emphasizing political guidance, secretary corps development, and stricter enforcement of intra-Party regulations including the Regulations on Party Building in Military Party Organizations and the Outline for Grassroots Building in the Military. The article documents a persistent institutional problem the PLA and PAP have acknowledged repeatedly: political control mechanisms attenuate at the unit level, and the 'nerve endings' of Party authority do not reliably transmit CMC directives down to individual officers and soldiers. The PAP Research Institute authorship fits a pattern of the armed police system publishing normative political-work content that tracks CMC priorities—here, the 'Two Establishments' and CMC Chairman responsibility system—and its value is as a baseline record of how the institution frames the grassroots control problem and the specific remedies (secretary corps professionalization, democratic-centralism balance, system-based accountability) it is currently emphasizing.
Translation

Forge Strong and Powerful Grassroots Party Organizations

■ Zhang Yao, You Jingwen

Military grassroots Party organizations, as the "nerve endings" of the Party's body, are the foundation of all the Party's work and combat effectiveness in the armed forces. Chairman Xi has emphasized: "We must attach great importance to strengthening grassroots Party organization building, forge every grassroots Party organization into a strong fighting fortress, and drive comprehensive progress and across-the-board toughening (全面过硬) in grassroots building." To achieve the centenary goal of army building (建军一百年奋斗目标) on schedule and advance the modernization of national defense and the armed forces with high quality, we must place grassroots Party organization building in a position of importance, further strengthen its political functions and organizational functions, and ensure that the Party's absolute leadership over the armed forces reaches directly to the grassroots and directly to officers and soldiers.

Strengthen political guidance. Strong and powerful grassroots Party organizations must be capable of exercising political guidance, running the Party's will and positions through every domain and the entire process of force building, and ensuring that officers and soldiers unwaveringly listen to the Party and follow the Party. We must persistently use Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era to unite hearts and forge souls (凝心铸魂), thoroughly implement Xi Jinping's thought on strengthening the military (习近平强军思想), guide officers and soldiers to deeply comprehend the decisive significance of the "Two Establishments" (两个确立), strengthen the "Four Consciousnesses" (四个意识), firm up the "Four Confidences" (四个自信), achieve the "Two Upholds" (两个维护), implement the system of CMC Chairman responsibility (军委主席负责制), and ensure that they always maintain a high degree of alignment with the Party Central Committee, the Central Military Commission, and Chairman Xi in thought, politics, and action. We must drive the meticulous and thorough implementation of education systems, and in conjunction with the practice of political rectification training (政治整训), continuously reform study and improve study conduct (学风), guiding officers and soldiers to deeply comprehend the connotations and essential meaning of scientific theory and accurately grasp the positions, viewpoints, and methods embedded within it, thereby firmly establishing a scientific worldview, outlook on life, and values. Strengthen political tempering (政治历练): in conjunction with major exercise and training activities, urgent and difficult high-risk tasks, and similar opportunities, educate officers and soldiers to hold firm to their political stance and distinguish political right from wrong in complex environments, continuously improve political judgment (政治判断力), political comprehension (政治领悟力), and political execution (政治执行力), so that political capability matches and is suited to the political responsibilities they bear.

Consolidate organizational building. The Party's strength comes from organization; when organization is strong, the armed forces are strong. At the CMC Political Work Conference, Chairman Xi distilled and summarized the strategy of political army-building (政治建军方略) in the new era into "Ten Clarifications" (十个明确), one of which is "clarifying that the Party's strength comes from organization and that the cohesion and combat effectiveness of the forces come from organization." To consolidate organizational advantages, we must further optimize and standardize the establishment and operation of grassroots Party organizations. With the overall reshaping of the organizational structure of the People's Army, the systemic structure, type configuration, and functional allocation of our military's Party organizations have undergone correspondingly great changes. We must implement the Party's organizational line for the new era, further emancipate the mind and keep pace with the times, focus on new situations and new problems in grassroots Party organization building, practically identify the correct functional positioning, optimize organizational setup, improve institutional mechanisms, and reform leadership methods, continuously exploring standardized organizational setup and operational methods, and persistently weaving tighter and building stronger the Party's organizational system. Grasp the key of building a strong secretary corps (书记队伍): reinforce the orientation toward political solidity, outstanding capability, solid conduct, and clean self-discipline; through institutional training, mentoring and guidance, and on-the-job tempering, continuously elevate the organizational leadership capability and war-preparedness and combat capability of the secretary corps, using the "lead-goose effect" (头雁效应) to stimulate the "vitality of the flock" (群雁活力). Improve the deliberation and decision-making mechanisms of grassroots Party organizations: both fully develop intra-Party democracy and strengthen centralized and unified leadership; by maintaining close ties with officers and soldiers, relying firmly on officers and soldiers, and resolving the urgent, difficult, worrying, and anticipated problems (急难愁盼问题) of officers and soldiers, continuously strengthen the Party organization's cohesion, centripetal force, and combat effectiveness.

Strictly implement systems. Scientific systems are an important guarantee for the routine operation and effective functioning of grassroots Party organizations. We must take the strict implementation of intra-Party rules and regulations as an important handle for grassroots Party organization building; using regulations and systems such as the Regulations on Party Building in Military Party Organizations of the Chinese Communist Party (《中国共产党军队党的建设条例》) and the Outline for Grassroots Building in the Military (《军队基层建设纲要》) as the basis, continuously improve a scientific, complete, effective, and practical institutional system, so that all work in grassroots Party organization building has laws to rely on and is grounded in regulations. Strengthen rule-of-law awareness and reinforce rule-of-law thinking; organize Party member cadres to study intra-Party rules and regulations in depth, deeply recognize the important significance of institutional building, strengthen the political consciousness, ideological consciousness, and action consciousness of respecting, studying, abiding by, and applying the law, and cultivate the good habit of making decisions and conducting affairs in accordance with rules and regulations. Focus on rectifying problems: targeting the weak links and shortcomings in grassroots Party organization building, use institutional implementation to drive problem rectification, and use institutional innovation to resolve contradictions and difficulties, continuously elevating the level of standardization and normalization of grassroots Party organization building. Strengthen supervision and discipline enforcement: clarify supervisory responsibilities and refine supervisory measures; conduct routine supervisory inspections of the implementation of systems; strictly pursue accountability for situations where orders are not followed and prohibitions are not heeded; use the rigid constraints of systems to continuously drive quality improvement and increased effectiveness in grassroots Party organization building.

(Authors' unit: People's Armed Police Force Research Institute)

锻造坚强有力的基层党组织 ■张 瑶 尤靖雯 军队基层党组织作为党的肌体的“神经末梢”，是党在军队全部工作和战斗力的基础。习主席强调：“要高度重视加强基层党组织建设，把每个基层党组织都锻造成坚强战斗堡垒，推动基层建设全面进步、全面过硬。”如期实现建军一百年奋斗目标、高质量推进国防和军队现代化，必须把基层党组织建设摆在重要位置，进一步增强其政治功能和组织功能，确保党对军队的绝对领导直达基层、直达官兵。 强化政治引领。坚强有力的基层党组织必须能够发挥政治引领作用，将党的意志和主张贯穿到部队建设的各领域、全过程，确保官兵坚定不移听党话、跟党走。要坚持不懈用习近平新时代中国特色社会主义思想凝心铸魂，深入贯彻习近平强军思想，引导官兵深刻领悟“两个确立”的决定性意义，增强“四个意识”、坚定“四个自信”、做到“两个维护”，贯彻军委主席负责制，确保在思想上政治上行动上始终同党中央、中央军委和习主席保持高度一致。推动教育制度落细落实，结合政治整训实践，不断改造学习、改进学风，引导官兵深刻领悟科学理论的内涵要义，准确把握蕴含其中的立场观点方法，从而树牢科学的世界观人生观价值观。强化政治历练，结合重大演训活动、急难险重任务等契机，教育官兵在复杂环境中站稳政治立场、明辨政治是非，不断提高政治判断力、政治领悟力、政治执行力，使政治能力能够与担当的政治责任相匹配、相适应。 夯实组织建设。党的力量来自组织，组织强则军队强。在中央军委政治工作会议上，习主席将新时代政治建军方略凝练概括为“十个明确”，其中之一就是“明确党的力量来自组织、部队凝聚力战斗力来自组织”。强固组织优势，必须进一步优化规范基层党组织设置运行。随着人民军队组织架构整体重塑，我军党组织的体系结构、类型设置、职能配置等相应发生了很大变化。要贯彻新时代党的组织路线，进一步解放思想、与时俱进，着眼基层党组织建设的新情况新问题，切实找准职能定位、优化组织设置、健全制度机制、改进领导方式，不断探索规范组织设置和运行方式，持续织密建强党的组织体系。抓好建强书记队伍这个关键，强化政治过硬、能力突出、作风扎实、廉洁自律等导向，通过院校集训、帮带指导、岗位锻炼等方式，持续提升书记队伍的组织领导能力和备战打仗能力，以“头雁效应”激发“群雁活力”。完善基层党组织议事决策机制，既充分发扬党内民主，又强化集中统一领导，通过密切联系官兵、紧紧依靠官兵、解决官兵急难愁盼问题，不断增强党组织的凝聚力向心力战斗力。 严格制度落实。科学的制度是基层党组织常态运行、发挥作用的重要保证。要把严格落实党内法规制度作为基层党组织建设的重要抓手，以《中国共产党军队党的建设条例》《军队基层建设纲要》等法规制度为依据，不断完善科学完备、有效管用的制度体系，使基层党组织建设各项工作都有法可依、于法有据。增强法治意识、强化法治思维，组织党员干部深入学习党内法规制度，深刻认识制度建设的重要意义，增强尊法学法守法用法的政治自觉、思想自觉和行动自觉，养成按法规制度决策办事的良好习惯。聚焦问题整改，针对基层党组织建设中的短板弱项，以制度落实推动问题整改，以制度创新破解矛盾难题，不断提升基层党组织建设标准化、规范化水平。加强监督执纪，明确监督责任、细化监督措施，对制度落实情况开展常态化监督检查，对有令不行、有禁不止的情况要严肃追责问责，以制度的刚性约束推动基层党组织建设不断提质增效。 （作者单位：武警部队研究院）
