About this analysis
Summary
An officer in the People's Armed Police Beijing General Corps published a political education directive calling on unit commanders to modernize ideological indoctrination by deploying VR/AR technology, short-video platforms, and targeted 'drip irrigation' messaging to reach internet-native conscripts, while reinforcing loyalty to Xi Jinping through the 'Two Establishments' and 'Two Safeguards' formulations. The piece reflects the PAP's sustained effort to address a recognized vulnerability—declining receptiveness to traditional political work among younger enlisted personnel—by adapting propaganda delivery methods without altering doctrinal content. It is useful as evidence that the PLA and PAP are institutionalizing digital and immersive media tools within the political work system, not merely as modernization gestures but as responses to concrete concerns about ideological cohesion in the ranks.
Translation

Grasp the Characteristics of the Era, Carry Forward Glorious Traditions

■ Lu Jinwei

Glorious traditions are not memories sealed away in dust, but a spiritual torrent surging forth from the depths of history. Chairman Xi has pointed out: "We must take the lead in studying traditions, cherishing traditions, and speaking about traditions, and drive the officers and soldiers of our units to carry forward the red genes and preserve the character of the old Red Army." Under new historical conditions, how to properly carry forward the glorious traditions of our Party and our military, and allow the character of the old Red Army to radiate even more brilliant light, is an important question of the era that we must answer well.

Closely align with young officers and soldiers, and strive to manifest the characteristics of the era in glorious tradition education. Young officers and soldiers are the internet-native generation; only by dressing glorious traditions in "new clothes of the era," giving them a strong sense of contemporaneity and inspirational power, can we win over the officers and soldiers. Persist in precise drip irrigation (精准滴灌), and abandon the preachiness of "flood irrigation" (大水漫灌). Officers and soldiers of the new era have active minds and distinct personalities; it is necessary to adapt to the era's characteristics of officers and soldiers, focus on real-world problems of ideology and conduct, and carry out glorious tradition education in a targeted manner—extending classroom education into training intervals and frontline duty posts, transforming "force-feeding" into "interactive" methods, and allowing officers and soldiers to receive edification through precise drip irrigation. Persist in equality and mutual trust, and cultivate a new type of officer-soldier relationship. The officer-soldier relationship in the new era faces erosion from unhealthy social tendencies; it is essential to strengthen the concept of putting the grassroots first and soldiers first, resolutely eliminate official-rank-centered thinking (官本位思想), open up democratic channels, allow soldiers to dare to speak their minds and dare to report their difficulties, and continuously consolidate internal relations that are united, friendly, harmonious, and pure. Persist in rule-of-law thinking, and leave behind the inertia of "rule by man." Under new historical conditions, the traditional "strict compliance with orders" (令行禁止) requires rule-of-law thinking as its guarantee. We must educate and guide officers and soldiers to improve their ability to use rule-of-law thinking and rule-of-law methods to carry out work and resolve problems, and continuously strengthen the awareness of handling affairs according to law, turning to law when matters arise, using law to resolve problems, and relying on law to defuse contradictions.

Closely align with the development of the era, and expand the methods and pathways for carrying forward glorious traditions. If one cannot pass the test of the internet, one cannot pass the test of the era. If glorious tradition education remains only in the display boards of honor rooms and the text of company history books, it will be difficult to reach the hearts of officers and soldiers. We must persist in upholding the correct course while innovating (守正创新), carry forward glorious traditions in ways that officers and soldiers enjoy and welcome, make sealed-away memories "come alive," and make the echoes of history "ignite." Emphasize immersive experience, and build red cloud classrooms. Use modern technologies such as VR (virtual reality) and AR (augmented reality) to recreate historical scenes, and through role-playing, scene reenactment, and other methods, allow officers and soldiers to feel as though they are present, entering the battlefields of the past—cultivating loyalty and martial spirit (忠诚血性) by performing as heroes and learning from heroes, and receiving spiritual baptism through dialogues that transcend time and space, achieving the effect of education penetrating the mind and heart. Expand the new-media matrix, and occupy the main position on the internet. In response to the reality of officers and soldiers each having "one person, one device, one world," micro-videos, micro-films, comics, and other means can be used to bring glorious tradition education into public accounts, short-video platforms, and military camp broadcasts—using positive energy to leverage large traffic flows, allowing glorious traditions to flood the "Moments" feed, and achieving viral dissemination (裂变式传播) through "likes" and "shares." Strengthen ceremonial education, and provide edification in a way that moistens things silently (润物无声). Improve methods of ceremonial education; for example, at rank-conferral ceremonies and oath-taking rallies, multimedia technology can be used to present the glorious combat history of the company, tempering the resolve and courage of officers and soldiers to dare to fight and be certain of victory. At the same time, persist in integrating the study, recounting, and application of traditions into daily life; through building sculptures of heroes and models, walls of honor, and similar measures, regularly organize officers and soldiers to carry out activities such as studying heroes and models and selecting advanced exemplars, so that officers and soldiers, in a way that moistens things silently, externalize glorious traditions into conscious action.

Closely align with the mission and tasks, and put glorious traditions into concrete action. Glorious traditions are precious spiritual wealth formed under specific historical conditions; their vitality lies in resonating in step with the era. Only by closely combining the spiritual core of glorious traditions with the mission and tasks of the people's military in the new era can we resolve the practical problems faced in carrying forward glorious traditions in the new era. Manifest the political consciousness of ready compliance with orders (听令景从). Loyalty to the Party is the unshakable political conviction of revolutionary soldiers; we must make officers and soldiers understand that loyalty in the new era is reflected not only in political steadfastness when confronting ideological struggles, but also in the conscious action of firmly upholding the "Two Establishments" (两个确立) and resolutely achieving the "Two Safeguards" (两个维护). Manifest the sense of mission and responsibility to safeguard social peace and order. From the "Three Main Rules of Discipline and Eight Points for Attention" (三大纪律八项注意) of the wartime era to supporting the government and cherishing the people in peacetime, serving the people wholeheartedly has always been the fundamental purpose of our military; today, the connotation of this tradition has been concretized as the mission and responsibility to safeguard social peace and order. We must make officers and soldiers understand that patrolling and guarding the borders when ten thousand families are reunited, and stepping forward during disaster relief operations, are all writing the new era's "bond between the military and the people" (军民鱼水情). Manifest the dedication and perseverance of finding joy in hardship (以苦为乐). Living conditions in the military have greatly improved, but the spirit of hard struggle must not be abandoned. We must make officers and soldiers understand that the more favorable the conditions, the more vigilant one must be against ideological laxity; maintaining a mental state of finding joy in hardship and always preserving one's true character in the face of various temptations of material interests—this is hard struggle in the new era.

(Author's unit: People's Armed Police Beijing General Corps)

把握时代特点 弘扬光荣传统 ■卢金伟 优良传统不是尘封的记忆，而是从历史深处奔涌而来的精神洪流。习主席指出：“要带头学传统、爱传统、讲传统，带动部队官兵传承好红色基因、保持老红军本色。”新的历史条件下，如何弘扬好我党我军光荣传统，让老红军本色焕发出更璀璨的光芒，是我们必须答好的一道重要时代课题。 紧贴青年官兵，努力彰显光荣传统教育的时代特征。青年官兵是网生一代，唯有让光荣传统穿上“时代新衣”，使其具有强烈的时代感和感召力，才能赢得官兵。坚持精准滴灌，摒弃“大水漫灌”的说教。新时代官兵思想活跃、个性鲜明，需适应官兵时代特点、聚焦现实思想作风问题有针对性地开展光荣传统教育，把课堂教育延伸到训练间隙、执勤一线，变“填鸭式”为“互动式”，让官兵在精准滴灌中接受熏陶。坚持平等互信，培塑新型官兵关系。新时代官兵关系，面临着社会不良风气的侵蚀，必须强化基层至上、士兵第一的理念，坚决破除官本位思想，畅通民主渠道，让战士有话敢说、有难敢诉，不断巩固团结、友爱、和谐、纯洁的内部关系。坚持法治思维，告别“人治”惯性。新的历史条件下，传统的“令行禁止”需要法治思维来保障。要教育引导官兵提高运用法治思维和法治方式开展工作、解决问题的能力，不断强化办事依法、遇事找法、解决问题用法、化解矛盾靠法的意识。 紧贴时代发展，拓展弘扬光荣传统的方法路径。过不了网络关，就过不了时代关。光荣传统教育如果仅仅停留在荣誉室里的展板、连史册里的文字，就很难走进官兵内心。必须坚持守正创新，用官兵喜闻乐见的方式弘扬光荣传统，让尘封的记忆“活”起来，让历史的回响“燃”起来。注重沉浸式体验，打造红色云课堂。借助VR（虚拟现实）、AR（增强现实）等现代技术还原历史场景，通过角色扮演、情景再现等方式，让官兵身临其境，走进当年的战场，在演英雄、学英雄中培育忠诚血性，在穿越时空的对话中接受心灵洗礼，达到教育入脑入心的效果。拓展新媒体矩阵，占领网络主阵地。针对官兵“一人一机一世界”的现状，可通过微视频、微电影、漫画等手段，把光荣传统教育搬进公众号、短视频平台、军营广播中，以正能量撬动大流量，让光荣传统在“朋友圈”刷屏，在“点赞”和“转发”中实现裂变式传播。强化仪式教育，于润物无声中熏陶。要改进仪式教育方法，如在授衔仪式、誓师大会上可借助多媒体技术呈现连队光荣战史，砥砺官兵敢打必胜的决心勇气。同时，坚持把学传统、讲传统、用传统融入日常，通过建设英模雕塑、荣誉墙等，常态组织官兵开展学习英模、评选先进典型等活动，让官兵于润物无声中将光荣传统外化为自觉行动。 紧贴使命任务，把光荣传统落于具体行动。光荣传统是特定的历史环境下形成的宝贵精神财富，其生命力在于与时代同频共振。只有将光荣传统的精神内核与新时代人民军队的使命任务紧密结合，才能解决好在新时代赓续光荣传统所面临的现实问题。彰显为听令景从的政治自觉。对党忠诚是革命军人不可动摇的政治信念，要让官兵明白，新时代的忠诚不仅体现在面对意识形态斗争时的政治定力上，也体现在坚定拥护“两个确立”、坚决做到“两个维护”的自觉行动上。彰显为守护社会安宁的使命担当。从战争年代的“三大纪律八项注意”到和平时期的拥政爱民，全心全意为人民服务始终是我军的根本宗旨，如今这一传统的内涵已具化为守护社会安宁的使命担当。要让官兵明白，万家团圆时的巡逻戍边、抢险救灾时的挺身而出，都是在书写新时代的“军民鱼水情”。彰显为以苦为乐的奉献坚守。现在部队生活条件极大改善，但艰苦奋斗的精神不能丢。要让官兵明白，越是条件优越，越要警惕思想松懈，保持以苦为乐的精神状态，面对各种物质利益诱惑始终保持本色，就是新时代的艰苦奋斗。 （作者单位：武警北京总队）
