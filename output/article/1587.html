Military Units at All Levels Conduct In-Depth Study and Education on Establishing and Practicing the Correct Outlook on Political Achievement

军队各级深入开展树立和践行正确政绩观学习教育

About this analysis
Summary
PLA units across all major services and commands — including an Air Force brigade, a Rocket Force brigade, an Information Support Force unit, a Military Space Force unit, a Cyberspace Force unit, a Central Theater Command directly subordinate unit, a Western Theater Command directly subordinate unit, a Navy unit, a PAP Shanxi General Corps brigade, and a unit stationed in Xinjiang — are conducting a PLA-wide study and education campaign on the "correct outlook on political achievement" (正确政绩观), focused on ideological rectification, problem identification, and linking political work to warfighting readiness. This is standard political work content tied to a named campaign; its value is as a record of how the institution is operationalizing Xi's political achievement framework across service branches simultaneously, including the newly restructured Information Support Force, Military Space Force, and Cyberspace Force, confirming that these reorganized forces are being integrated into PLA-wide political education cycles on the same timeline as legacy services. The explicit linkage of the campaign to combat effectiveness metrics — particularly in the Central Theater, Western Theater, and Navy entries — documents the institutional effort to frame political compliance as a warfighting input rather than a separate administrative function.
Translation

Military Units at All Levels Conduct In-Depth Study and Education on Establishing and Practicing the Correct Outlook on Political Achievement — Continuously Consolidating Responsibility and Driving Thorough Implementation

■ PLA Daily Reporter Yang Lun

At present, study and education on establishing and practicing the correct outlook on political achievement (正确政绩观) is being carried out in a solid and orderly manner. Military units at all levels are conducting in-depth study and implementation of the spirit of Chairman Xi's important speeches and important instructions, persisting in treating the study and education as an important political task, tightening and consolidating Party committee leadership responsibilities, and continuously applying effort to deepening study and discussion, thoroughly identifying and pinpointing problems, and carrying out precise and practical rectification, so as to drive the study and education to achieve concrete results.

Grasping Ideological Rectification (思想整风) in Earnest and Tempering Party Character Cultivation

The Party committee of an Air Force brigade placed emphasis on transforming thinking to conduct study and discussion in earnest. Centering on deep study and comprehension of the spirit of Chairman Xi's important speech at the opening ceremony of the senior cadre training course for the entire military, leading group members took the lead in carrying out study exchanges and providing guidance, deepened theoretical study in conjunction with Party committee theoretical study center group sessions, the "three meetings and one lesson" (三会一课), and other occasions, and excised ideological impurities from the depths of the soul and set right what had been inverted and distorted.

The Party committee of a Rocket Force brigade placed emphasis on making good use of the "two mirrors" of positive and negative model cases. It mobilized Party member cadres to earnestly study the advanced deeds of Comrade Yang Yegong to conduct thorough positive education, used cases of violations of discipline and law within the force to strengthen mirror-reflection and self-examination, and carried out study and discussion centered on "examining initial aspirations, examining responsibilities, examining values, and displaying actual performance" (问初心、问职责、问价值、展实绩), guiding Party member cadres to know shame and know limits, and to keep vigilance in mind and act with discipline.

The Party committee of a unit under the Information Support Force conducted in-depth inspection and analysis, demanding that individuals, events, and thinking all be made visible. Benchmarking against the key problems to be resolved through the study and education, leading group members participated in dual organizational life (双重组织生活) as ordinary Party members, took the lead in conducting inspection, identification, and reflection, and in openly exposing their thinking, driving Party member cadres not to dilute, not to evade, and not to conceal shortcomings, and to rigorously inspect and reflect on problems at the root of their thinking.

The Party committee of a medical center under the PLA General Hospital persisted in running study and discussion throughout the entire course of the study and education. Adopting methods such as leadership-led study, collective research study, and focused supplementary study, it conducted in-depth study and discussion of the prescribed content, organized Party member cadres to share and exchange study insights collectively, carried out Party class guidance and explanatory lectures in a solid manner, and drove the correct outlook on political achievement to be internalized in the heart and externalized in action.

Persisting in Deep Inspection and Close Examination and Firmly Establishing Problem Targets

The Party committee of an Army group army strictly controlled the review and filing of problem lists. In conjunction with thematic organizational life inspection and analysis and the concentrated rectification of the prominent problem of new officials refusing to address old accounts (新官不理旧账), it dynamically adjusted the problem list, urged and guided subordinate units to review and file from the bottom up, and sternly urged and corrected instances where problems were identified insufficiently or inaccurately, genuinely ensuring that problems are identified precisely, measures are set concretely, and responsibilities are tightened.

The Party committee of a unit under the Military Space Force listened to opinions and suggestions from officers and soldiers through multiple channels including paired assistance and construction visits and inspection research trips. Relying on the office network, it established a "direct line to the grassroots" (基层直通车) to collect officers' and soldiers' demands in a timely manner, formulated and implemented a problem list, and the Party committee organ held concentrated consultations each quarter to supervise and advance items where rectification was lagging.

The Party committee of a unit under the Cyberspace Force persisted in linking organizational inspection with individual inspection and higher-level supervision with lower-level reporting, conducting comprehensive and in-depth identification of problems. It posted the problem list and rectification measures on the office network for officers and soldiers to supervise, and simultaneously established mechanisms for pending-account supervision, closed-account management, and regular notification, working hard to drive problem resolution.

Leading group members of the Party committee of a brigade under the People's Armed Police Shanxi General Corps persisted in placing themselves within the frame of inspection and problem identification, taking the lead in conducting genuine, thorough, and deep inspection. Centering on the key problems to be resolved through the study and education, they carefully examined shortcomings and deficiencies in work guidance, performance of duties, spirit of responsibility, and ideological conduct. The resulting problem list was reviewed and verified by the secretary and deputy secretary.

Emphasizing Using Study to Promote Action and Driving War Preparation and Difficult Breakthroughs

The Party committee of a directly subordinate unit of the Central Theater Command persisted in establishing and practicing the correct outlook on political achievement in the course of advancing primary responsibilities and main tasks. It took whether something is conducive to improving combat effectiveness as the fundamental measure of work quality and effectiveness, sorted through and inventoried problems discovered in support of exercises and training, refined the identification and correction of deviations in the outlook on political achievement in the area of military training and war preparation, and pooled wisdom to break through real and urgent problems in the operational domain.

The Party committee of a directly subordinate unit of the Western Theater Command used the correct outlook on political achievement to formulate and execute plans and programs. Centering on improving the contribution rate of service and support to the main combat mission, it researched and formulated a five-year capability generation plan, demonstrated and established key engineering projects, developed a "project management system" to dynamically supervise project progress, and used project implementation to drive the generation of warfighting capability.

The Party committee of a Navy unit placed emphasis on calibrating the "political achievement yardstick" against victory in battle. Focusing on resolving capability shortfalls in adapting to the situation and tasks, it organized thematic discussions on warfare and training, carried out "study, apply, train, and assess" (学、用、训、考) activities closely aligned with operational tasks, operational adversaries, and the operational environment, and followed up on researching and revising operational plans in conjunction with exercises and training, continuously tempering strong capabilities and improving warfighting ability.

The Party committee of a unit stationed in Xinjiang advanced the "14th Five-Year" (十五五) planning and construction with a scientific and pragmatic attitude. Reflecting on and drawing lessons from past shortcomings of insufficient overall coordination and weak system coherence in plan formulation, it assigned leading group members to head demonstration and research efforts across different domains, broke down multiple projects and organized expert teams to conduct in-depth consultation and assessment, and the Party committee conducted multiple concentrated reviews and checks, scientifically formulating a construction roadmap for the unit's high-quality development.

军队各级深入开展树立和践行正确政绩观学习教育—— 持续压实责任 推动走深走实 ■解放军报记者 杨 伦 当前，树立和践行正确政绩观学习教育正在扎实有序开展。军队各级深入学习贯彻习主席重要讲话和重要指示精神，坚持把学习教育作为重要政治任务，压紧压实党委领导责任，在深化学习研讨、查清找准问题、精准务实整改上持续用力，推动学习教育取得实实在在成效。 抓实思想整风 锤炼党性修养 空军某旅党委突出改造思想抓实学习研讨，围绕深学深悟习主席在全军高级干部培训班开班式上的重要讲话精神，班子成员带头开展学习交流、搞好领学辅导，结合党委理论学习中心组学习、“三会一课”等时机深化理论学习，从灵魂深处剔除思想杂质、把颠倒扭曲的东西正过来。 火箭军某旅党委注重用好正反面典型案例“两面镜子”，发动党员干部认真学习杨业功同志先进事迹深抓正面教育，运用部队违纪违法案例强化镜鉴自省，围绕“问初心、问职责、问价值、展实绩”开展学习讨论，引导党员干部知耻知止、警心策行。 信息支援部队某部党委深入检视剖析，务求见人见事见思想，对照学习教育重点解决的问题，班子成员以普通党员身份参加双重组织生活，带头检视查摆反思、晾晒曝光思想，带动党员干部不淡化、不回避、不遮丑，从严检视反思思想根子上的问题。 解放军总医院某医学中心党委坚持将学习研讨贯穿学习教育始终，采取领导领学、集体研学、重点辅学等方式，深入抓好规定内容的学习研讨，组织党员干部集中分享交流学习感悟，扎实开展党课辅导、宣讲解读，推动正确政绩观内化于心、外化于行。 坚持深查细照 立牢问题靶标 陆军某集团军党委严把问题清单审核备案，结合专题组织生活检视剖析、集中整治新官不理旧账突出问题，动态调整问题清单，督促指导所属部队自下而上审核备案，对查摆问题不深不实的严厉督促纠正，切实把问题找准、措施定实、责任压紧。 军事航天部队某部党委结合挂钩帮建、检查调研等多种渠道听取官兵意见建议，依托办公网建立“基层直通车”及时收集官兵诉求，制订落实问题清单，党委机关每季度集中会商、督办推进整改迟缓事项。 网络空间部队某部党委坚持组织查与个人查、上级督与下级提相贯通，全面深入查摆问题，把问题清单和整改措施晒在办公网上、接受官兵监督，同步建立挂账督办、销号管理、定期通报等机制，着力推动问题解决。 武警山西总队某支队党委班子成员坚持把自己摆进去检视查摆问题，带头真查实查深查，围绕学习教育重点解决的问题，认真审视工作指导、履职状态、担当精神和思想作风方面的差距不足，形成的问题清单由书记、副书记审核把关。 注重以学促干 推动备战攻坚 中部战区某直属单位党委坚持在推进主责主业中树立和践行正确政绩观，将是否有利于提高战斗力作为衡量工作质效的根本标尺，梳理盘点支撑保障演习演训发现的问题，细化查纠练兵备战方面的政绩观偏差，集智破解作战领域的现实紧迫问题。 西部战区某直属单位党委以正确政绩观编制执行规划计划，围绕提升服务支撑主战贡献率，研究制订5年能力生成计划，论证设置重点工程项目，开发“项目管理系统”动态监管项目推进情况，以项目落地推动打赢能力生成。 海军某部党委注重把“政绩标尺”卡在胜战打赢上，聚焦适应形势任务解决能力短板组织专题议战议训，紧贴作战任务、作战对手、作战环境开展“学、用、训、考”活动，结合演习演训跟进研案修案，不断锤炼过硬本领、提升打赢能力。 驻疆某部党委以科学务实态度推进“十五五”规划建设，反思镜鉴以往编制规划统筹不够、体系不强的教训，区分不同领域由班子成员牵头论证攻关，分解多个项目组织专家团队深入咨询评估，党委多次集中研究把关，科学制订部队高质量发展的施工图。
