Zhang Shengmin Attends Special Conference on Deepening the Arming of Minds with Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military and Delivers Remarks

张升民出席深化习近平强军思想武装专题会议并讲话

About this analysis
Summary
CMC Vice Chairman Zhang Shengmin convened a military-wide special conference in Beijing on May 27 on deepening ideological indoctrination with Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military, with department and unit leaders attending and conducting discussion. The conference foregrounded the 'Two Establishments' and 'Two Safeguards' formulas alongside CMC Chairman responsibility system compliance, framing the PLA's centenary year as a moment requiring renewed ideological consolidation — a pattern that extends the post-2023 political rectification campaign into the centenary mobilization cycle. The explicit call for 思想整风 (ideological rectification) and the emphasis on the 'key minority' of senior leaders documents institutional concern that theoretical compliance among the officer corps remains uneven and requires targeted enforcement rather than routine study.
Translation

Zhang Shengmin Stresses at Special Conference on Deepening the Arming of Minds with Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military: Thoroughly Study and Implement Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military; Welcome the Centenary of the Founding of the Army with a Completely New Political Bearing

PLA Daily, Beijing, May 27 — Reporters Yan Shan and Yan Zifeng report: A special conference on deepening the arming of minds with Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military (深化习近平强军思想武装专题会议) was convened in Beijing on the 27th. Zhang Shengmin, Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission, attended the conference and delivered remarks. He stressed that it is necessary to perseveringly arm the mind, guide practice, and drive work with Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era; to deepen theoretical arming (理论武装) with a higher political standpoint and solid, forceful measures; to firmly establish the guiding status of Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military in national defense and military building; and to drive the entire military to welcome the centenary of the founding of the Army with a completely new political bearing.

Zhang Shengmin pointed out that it is necessary to comprehensively and accurately study and grasp, and unwaveringly implement, Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military; to guide officers and soldiers throughout the military to deeply comprehend the decisive significance of the "Two Establishments" (两个确立); to resolutely achieve the "Two Safeguards" (两个维护); to implement the system of CMC Chairman responsibility (军委主席负责制); and to ensure that the People's Army always strives to strengthen the military under the Party's banner. It is necessary to uphold the unity of arming the mind and transforming thinking (武装思想与改造思想相统一), to earnestly carry out ideological rectification (思想整风), to use the Party's innovative theories as a weapon to transform the subjective world, to solidly lay the foundation of ideals and convictions, and to forge the political soul of loyalty to the Party. It is necessary to promote the study style of integrating theory with practice, to master scientific standpoints, viewpoints, and methods, to update ways of thinking and concepts, and to achieve solid results in resolving contradictions and problems and in advancing construction and development.

Zhang Shengmin stressed that Party committees at all levels must shoulder the political responsibility of managing ideology and grasping study; must highlight the "key minority" (关键少数) in improving methods and models for organizing and conducting study; must closely follow the ideological pulse of officers and soldiers to improve the quality and effectiveness of forging the soul and cultivating people (铸魂育人); must strengthen theoretical publicity and research and interpretation; and must drive theoretical arming to go deeper and be more grounded. Leading cadres must take the lead in studying and take the lead in acting, driving the formation of a strong atmosphere of genuine study and genuine action, real effort and solid work.

Relevant leaders from various departments and units of the military attended the conference and conducted discussion and exchange.
