Zhang Shengmin Emphasizes at Thematic Conference on Deepening Armament with Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military: Thoroughly Study and Implement Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military, and Welcome the Centenary of the Founding of the Army with a Wholly New Political Bearing

About this analysis
Summary
CMC Vice Chairman Zhang Shengmin convened a military-wide thematic conference in Beijing on May 27 to drive ideological consolidation ahead of the PLA's centenary in August 2027, framing the campaign around the 'Two Establishments,' 'Two Safeguards,' and CMC Chairman responsibility system. The conference documents the institutional machinery the PLA uses to synchronize political loyalty messaging across the force at major symbolic milestones — the centenary framing here is doing real work, not decoration, as it sets a deadline-driven standard ('wholly new political bearing') against which unit-level compliance will be measured. The emphasis on 'unifying the arming of thought with the transformation of thought' extends a recurring CMC concern that study campaigns produce performative rather than genuine ideological conformity.
Translation

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 27 (Reporter Mei Changwei) — A thematic conference on deepening armament with Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military (深化习近平强军思想武装专题会议) was held in Beijing on the 27th. Central Military Commission Vice Chairman Zhang Shengmin attended the conference and delivered remarks. He emphasized the need to persistently arm the mind with, guide practice with, and advance work through Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era; to comprehensively and accurately study and grasp, and unwaveringly implement, Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military; and to drive the entire military to welcome the centenary of the founding of the army with a wholly new political bearing (崭新政治面貌).

Zhang Shengmin noted that efforts must be made to continuously deepen theoretical armament (理论武装), uphold the unity of arming thought and transforming thought (武装思想与改造思想相统一), consolidate the foundation of ideals and convictions, connect theory to reality to resolve problems and promote development, and translate the results of study into concrete action in firmly upholding the "Two Establishments" (两个确立), resolutely achieving the "Two Safeguards" (两个维护), and implementing the system of CMC Chairman responsibility (军委主席负责制). The political responsibility of managing thought and grasping study must be shouldered; the model for organizing and conducting study must be improved; the quality and effectiveness of forging the soul and cultivating people (铸魂育人) must be raised; propaganda and elucidation must be strengthened; and theoretical armament must be driven to go deeper and be more substantive.

Relevant leaders from various departments and units of the military attended the conference and took part in discussion and exchange.

Original Chinese
新华社北京5月27日电（记者梅常伟）深化习近平强军思想武装专题会议27日在京召开。中央军委副主席张升民出席会议并讲话。他强调，要坚持不懈用习近平新时代中国特色社会主义思想武装头脑、指导实践、推动工作，全面准确学习领会、毫不动摇贯彻落实习近平强军思想，推动全军以崭新政治面貌迎接建军一百周年。 张升民指出，要持续深化理论武装，坚持武装思想与改造思想相统一，筑牢理想信念之基，联系实际破解问题、促进发展，把学习成效转化为坚定拥护“两个确立”、坚决做到“两个维护”，贯彻军委主席负责制的实际行动。要扛起管思想抓学习的政治责任，改进组学抓学模式，提高铸魂育人质效，加强宣传阐释，推动理论武装走深走实。 军队各部门各单位有关领导参加会议并作研讨交流。
