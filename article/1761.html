About this analysis
Summary
From June 25 to July 1, the Ministry of National Defense is hosting the 2nd SCO Junior-and-Middle-Level Officers' Exchange Activity, organized by the National University of Defense Technology and drawing junior officers and defense officials from SCO member states, observer states, dialogue partners, and the SCO Secretariat, with programming centered on lectures, seminars, and site visits. The event documents China's use of NUDT as an institutional vehicle for multilateral military socialization within the SCO framework, extending a pattern in which PLA-affiliated academic institutions host foreign junior officers to build long-term professional relationships before those officers reach senior command positions. The second iteration of this format confirms it has moved from pilot to recurring program, raising the question of how participation breadth and country composition have shifted since the first exchange.
Translation

Ministry of National Defense Hosts the 2nd Shanghai Cooperation Organization Junior-and-Middle-Level Officers' Exchange Activity MND Will Host the 2nd SCO Junior-and-Middle-Level Officers' Exchange

On the afternoon of June 25, the Ministry of National Defense held its regular press conference, at which Ministry of National Defense Spokesperson Senior Colonel Zhang Xiaogang answered questions from reporters.

Zhang Xiaogang: From June 25 to July 1, the Ministry of National Defense is hosting the 2nd Shanghai Cooperation Organization Junior-and-Middle-Level Officers' Exchange Activity, inviting junior-and-middle-level officers and defense officials from SCO member states, observer states, dialogue partners, and relevant countries, as well as representatives from the SCO Secretariat, to participate. This activity is organized by the National University of Defense Technology, with the theme "SCO Youth Together, Jointly Building a Secure Future" (上合青年同行，共筑安全未来). It will carry out activities including thematic lectures, seminars and exchanges, and visits and tours. It aims to deepen the foundations of friendship, build consensus on cooperation, and actively promote high-quality development of SCO defense and security cooperation that is more united (更加团结), more collaborative (更重协作), more dynamic (更富活力), and more capable of achieving results (更有作为).

Zhang Xiaogang: The MND will host the 2nd SCO Junior-and-Middle-Level Officers' Exchange from June 25 to July 1. The event will bring together junior-and-middle-level officers and defense officials from SCO member states, observer states, dialogue partners and relevant countries, as well as representatives from the SCO Secretariat. Themed "SCO Youth Together for a Safe Future", the activity will be organized by the PLA National University of Defense Technology, and include such activities as themed lectures, seminars and discussions, and visits. It aims to consolidate friendship, build consensus on cooperation, and actively promote high-quality development of SCO defense and security cooperation featuring greater solidarity, coordination, vitality and influence.
