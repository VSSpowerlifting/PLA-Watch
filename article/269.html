A PLA Navy Unit Conducts In-Depth Study and Implementation of Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military——

Promoting Action Through Study, Forging the Sword Across Sea and Sky

■ PLA Daily Special Correspondents Yu Hang and Fu Jinquan

"We must implement the military strategic guidelines for the new era, uphold combat effectiveness as the sole and fundamental standard, focus all energy on fighting wars, direct all work toward fighting wars, and accelerate the improvement of the ability to win."

Not long ago, a flight training session at a PLA Navy unit had just concluded when an after-action review and discussion immediately got underway. Officers and soldiers engaged in spirited discussion centered on the important statements in "Xi Jinping on Strengthening and Developing the Military (Volume IV)" regarding accelerating the improvement of the force's modernization level and ability to win, and in conjunction with actual training conditions, identified problems and formulated improvement measures for the next phase.

"Advancing the cause of strengthening the military in the new era requires the correct guidance of scientific theory." The unit's leadership introduced that they have incorporated the study of "Xi Jinping on Strengthening and Developing the Military (Volume IV)" as a key component, integrating it into the Party committee's theoretical study center group sessions, cadre theoretical rotation training, and the unit's ideological and political education plan. They have adopted a model of Party committee-led study, grassroots research-based study, and individual self-study to help officers and soldiers deeply study and carefully comprehend the military philosophy and methodology contained in Xi Jinping's Thought on Strengthening the Military. During study, members of the unit Party committee standing body took the lead in reading the original works, studying the original texts, and internalizing the original principles, conducting focused discussions on themes including political development of the military (政治建军) and system-of-systems operations (体系作战); organs relied on the Strong Military Network (强军网) to build an online study platform for the convenience of officers and soldiers in timely study; and grassroots units used intervals between training sessions and other opportunities to organize officers and soldiers to share experiences and reflections in connection with actual mission conditions, genuinely improving the quality and effectiveness of study.

"The entire purpose of study lies in application." The unit's leadership stated that they have combined theoretical study with solving difficult training problems, guiding officers and soldiers to put effort into truly understanding, thoroughly grasping, and faithfully implementing, and striving to transform the results of study into a powerful force for guiding practice and driving work forward. In practice, the unit requires leading cadres to proactively take on assigned topics and conduct in-depth research into the tactics and training methods for key and difficult subjects; it organizes officers and soldiers to conduct discussions and analysis centered on combat-realistic subjects from the standpoint of their own posts, guiding everyone to deepen their understanding through specific tasks and consciously apply the Party's innovative theories to solve the difficult problems of training troops and preparing for war; and it emphasizes integrating the glorious history of battle into theoretical study, educating officers and soldiers to draw the strength to forge ahead from the deeds of their predecessors, and further solidifying their conviction to render meritorious service at their posts.

The deeper the study, the more resolute the action. Not long ago, a certain brigade (大队) of the unit organized night training. Confronting challenges including the high difficulty of carrier-based aircraft takeoffs and landings and the complex operational environment in distant seas, officers and soldiers were full of fighting spirit. Keeping their sights fixed on the demands of the future battlefield, they concentrated their efforts on tackling key and difficult subjects such as night carrier landings and coordinated strikes in complex electromagnetic environments, systematically refined multiple tactical methods and approaches, and concretely advanced the transformation of study results into combat effectiveness.

Promoting action through study, forging the sword across sea and sky. In recent days, from airport runways to distant-sea airspace, from command shelters to the front lines of support, the unit's officers and soldiers have concretely transformed their enthusiasm for study into a powerful driving force for training troops and preparing for war, continuously generating a rising tide of combat-realistic training.

海军某部深入学习贯彻习近平强军思想—— 以学促干 砺剑海天 ■解放军报特约记者 于 航 傅金泉 “要贯彻新时代军事战略方针，坚持战斗力这个唯一的根本的标准，全部精力向打仗聚焦，全部工作向打仗用劲，加快提高打赢能力。”日前，海军某部一场飞行训练刚结束，复盘研讨随即展开。官兵围绕《习近平论强军兴军（四）》中关于加快提高部队现代化水平和打赢能力的重要论述，进行热烈讨论，并结合训练实际查摆问题，制订下一步改进措施。 “推进新时代强军事业，需要科学理论的正确指引。”该部领导介绍，他们把学习《习近平论强军兴军（四）》作为重要内容，纳入党委理论学习中心组学习、干部理论轮训和部队思想政治教育计划，采取党委领学、基层研学、个人自学模式，帮助官兵深学细悟习近平强军思想蕴含的军事观和方法论。学习中，部党委班子成员带头读原著、学原文、悟原理，围绕政治建军、体系作战等主题开展集中研讨；机关依托强军网搭建线上学习平台，方便官兵及时学习；基层单位利用训练间隙等时机，组织官兵结合任务实际谈体会、话感悟，切实提升学习质效。 “学习的目的全在于运用。”该部领导表示，他们将理论学习与破解训练难题相结合，引导官兵在学懂弄通做实上下功夫，努力把学习成效转化为指导实践、推动工作的强大力量。实践中，该部要求领导干部主动领题，深研重难点科目战法训法；组织官兵立足本职岗位、围绕实战课题开展讨论辨析，引导大家在具体任务中深化理解认识，自觉用党的创新理论破解练兵备战难题；注重将光荣战史融入理论学习，教育官兵从先辈事迹中汲取奋进力量，进一步坚定岗位建功信念。 学习越深入，行动越坚定。前不久，该部某大队组织夜间训练。面对舰载机起降难度大、远海作战环境复杂等挑战，官兵斗志昂扬，紧盯未来战场需求，聚力攻关夜间着舰、复杂电磁环境下协同打击等重难点科目，针对性完善多项战术战法，切实推动学习成果向战斗力转化。 以学促干，砺剑海天。连日来，从机场跑道到远海空域，从指挥方舱到保障一线，该部官兵切实把学习热情转化为练兵备战的强大动力，持续掀起实战化训练热潮。
