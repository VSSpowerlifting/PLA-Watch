思想战线丨以理论上的清醒保证政治上的坚定

About this analysis
Summary
An article published under the byline of the 'Xi Jinping Thought on Strengthening the Military Research Center,' authored by Zhou Junjie, elaborates on Xi Jinping's directive—delivered at the opening ceremony of a senior cadre training course for the entire PLA—that 'theoretical clarity' in Marxist doctrine is the prerequisite for 'political steadfastness,' and calls on senior military cadres to deepen study of Xi Jinping Thought on Strengthening the Military as the foundation of loyalty and combat effectiveness. The piece is a doctrinal elaboration rather than a policy announcement, but it signals continued emphasis on ideological conformity as a command-and-control mechanism within the PLA, framing theoretical indoctrination explicitly as a counter to 'Western hostile forces' and internal wavering amid what the text describes as an unprecedented convergence of reform pressures and external strategic competition. The invocation of the Yan'an Rectification Movement as a model, combined with the targeting of senior cadres specifically, is consistent with the ongoing pattern of using centralized political education campaigns to enforce loyalty at the officer corps level.
Translation

Editor's Note: Not long ago, Chairman Xi profoundly observed at the opening ceremony of the senior cadre training course for the entire military that "we must earnestly study the Party's innovative theories, master the Marxist positions, viewpoints, and methods that run through them, and ensure political steadfastness through theoretical clarity." This important formulation is a scientific and refined summation of the laws governing ideological Party-building and political military-building (思想建党、政治建军), and it profoundly illuminates the dialectical relationship between theoretical clarity and political steadfastness. Military Party member cadres, especially senior cadres, must deeply comprehend the essential spirit of Chairman Xi's important address, consciously strengthen their grounding in the Party's innovative theories, and continuously drive forward the cause of building a strong military.

Ensuring Political Steadfastness Through Theoretical Clarity

— Studying and Implementing the Spirit of Chairman Xi's Important Address at the Opening Ceremony of the Senior Cadre Training Course for the Entire Military

■ Xi Jinping Thought on Strengthening the Military Research Center

Political steadfastness originates from theoretical clarity; theoretical maturity determines political maturity. For Party member cadres, theoretical clarity is the foundation of one's conduct and the basis of accomplishment — it is the fundamental prerequisite for holding firm to one's political stance, accurately grasping political direction, upholding political convictions, and strictly observing political discipline. At present, amid a complex and ever-changing domestic and international situation, and in the face of various risks, challenges, and the erosion of harmful ideologies, only by maintaining a high degree of theoretical clarity at all times — consolidating the foundation of conviction, replenishing the calcium of the spirit (补足精神之钙), and steadying the helm of thought — can one fulfill the founding mission and bravely shoulder the responsibilities of the era with rock-solid political resolve.

I

Ensuring political steadfastness through theoretical clarity is a scientific formulation grounded in the profound changes in the world situation, national conditions, Party conditions, and military conditions, and derived from the inherited historical experience of army-building and army-governance. It carries deep historical foundations and significant practical importance.

At present, the strategic overall situation of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation and the great changes unseen in a century are intersecting historically. From an international perspective, the characteristics of a period of global turbulence and transformation are pronounced; great-power competition is complex and intense; and the schemes of Western hostile forces to comprehensively contain, encircle, and suppress our country have never changed. The contest between forging the soul (铸魂) and corroding the soul (蛀魂), between consolidating the roots and destroying the roots, has not ceased for a single moment. From a domestic perspective, China's development has entered a period in which strategic opportunities and risks and challenges coexist, and uncertain and hard-to-predict factors are increasing. The People's Army is at a critical juncture in achieving the centenary goal of army-building (建军一百年奋斗目标). The weight of the tasks of reform, development, and stability, and the number of contradictions, risks, and challenges, are both unprecedented. Faced with a severe situation and contradictions and problems, if theoretical clarity is lacking, one will lack the political resolve to "sit steady in the fishing boat regardless of the rising waves" (任凭风浪起，稳坐钓鱼船). One will very easily lose direction and waver in one's stance on major matters of principle, and very likely fail to see through the essence or grasp the crux amid complex and tangled contradictions, ultimately impeding the smooth advancement of the Party's and the people's cause.

Looking back at the Party's history of struggle spanning more than a century, using theoretical innovation to lead practical exploration has run throughout. After the failure of the Great Revolution, the Party drew profound lessons from bloodshed and came to recognize that it was essential to integrate the basic principles of Marxism with China's specific realities, opening the correct path of "surrounding the cities from the countryside and seizing power through armed force" (农村包围城市、武装夺取政权), and driving the revolutionary spark to gradually become a prairie fire. The Yan'an Rectification Movement was a large-scale Marxist education movement across the entire Party. Through systematic study of theory, it settled accounts with subjectivism, sectarianism, and Party formalism (党八股), achieving unprecedented unity of the entire Party in ideology, politics, and organization, and laying a solid ideological and political foundation for winning the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation. Since the new era, every thematic education campaign (主题教育) conducted by our Party has invariably placed the study of theory first, using unified thinking to consolidate will and align steps. Successful practice demonstrates that persisting in arming the entire Party with scientific theory is the Party's precious experience in continuously overcoming difficulties, correcting errors, and advancing from victory to victory, and is the fundamental path for maintaining the Party's advanced nature, purity, and combat effectiveness.

For the People's Army, implementing the infusion of progressive thought (进步的思想贯注) and persisting in arming the force with scientific theory is the very source of this armed force's advanced nature. Consider: if our military lacks theoretical clarity, it is like losing guidance on the battlefield — if soldiers do not even know which direction to charge, how can they possibly win? As an important component of Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era, Xi Jinping Thought on Strengthening the Military is the latest achievement of the Party's military guiding theory and the action program for adhering to the path of strengthening the military with Chinese characteristics and comprehensively advancing the modernization of national defense and the military. Deeply studying and implementing this thought is the greatest theoretical clarity for our military. Only by studying deeply and comprehending thoroughly its core tenets, essential spirit, rich connotations, and practical requirements can one deeply understand the founding mission, glorious traditions, and the magic weapon for victory (制胜法宝) of this People's Army, and only then can one see through the essence and grasp the laws amid the complex and ever-changing transformations in science and technology, in warfare, and in adversaries, and forge the hard capabilities to build a strong military and win battles.

II

Ensuring political steadfastness through theoretical clarity contains rich philosophical principles and embodies profound laws, revealing from different dimensions the intrinsic connection between theory and politics.

It manifests the law governing the enhancement of political capability. When confronting major issues and at critical moments, whether one is clear-cut in one's position or ambiguous in one's attitude, whether one's stance is firm or wavering — at bottom, what is being tested is political capability. The cultivation of this capability is decidedly not the work of a single day; its foundation lies in deep theoretical grounding. Without the depth of theoretical thinking, one is easily misled by surface appearances and thus makes erroneous judgments. Only by systematically mastering Marxist positions, viewpoints, and methods can one see through phenomena to the essence, examine problems from a political perspective, and forge a pair of "fiery golden eyes" (火眼金睛). Many captured soldiers during the revolutionary war years were "not brave in the White Army yesterday, but very brave in the Red Army today" — this vividly illustrates that only when one is truly awakened theoretically can political and emotional identification be generated, which in turn transforms into political steadfastness. Therefore, theoretical clarity is often the intrinsic driving force for enhancing political judgment, political comprehension, and political execution, and it is what can transform scientific theory into a powerful material force for transforming the world.

It manifests the law governing the cultivation of Party spirit (党性修养). Ideals and convictions, as the political soul and spiritual pillar of Communist Party members and revolutionary soldiers, are the foundation of our existence. Our Party and our military are united by ideals and convictions. Why were so many revolutionary predecessors able to struggle their entire lives for their ideals and convictions, even at the cost of their lives? It was because through deep theoretical study they were firmly convinced that the cause they pursued was just and in accordance with the direction of historical development. Comrade Mao Zedong once said: "A doctrine is like a banner; only when the banner is raised do people have something to look toward and know where to go." This "doctrine" is conviction. If one does not truly study, understand, believe in, and apply Marxism, then ideals and convictions become water without a source and a tree without roots, liable to waver at the first encounter with hardship and obstacles. Only through systematic study of dialectical materialism and historical materialism, mastering the telescope and microscope for observing the world, can one stand firm in the face of trials by storm and stress, steady one's mind in the face of temptation and entrapment, maintain one's true character amid complex and severe struggles, and remain committed without wavering to the common ideal of socialism with Chinese characteristics.

It manifests the law governing rectification campaigns (整风整训规律). At every important historical juncture, our Party and our military have invariably used rectification campaigns to resolve prominent problems within the Party, ensuring the unity, purity, and honor of the ranks. The key to the success of the Yan'an Rectification Movement was placing theoretical study first. At the outset of the movement, the Party Central Committee clearly stipulated the required Marxist-Leninist classical works and central documents to be read, requiring the entire Party, especially senior cadres, to devote great effort to study. It was precisely because theory was studied one step ahead that everyone had a common "yardstick" for measuring right and wrong. The criticism and self-criticism that followed was an ideological contest guided by theory and grounded in facts. Through this method, everyone distinguished what was Marxism and what was subjectivism, what was proletarian thought and what was non-proletarian thought, thereby achieving the goals of "learning from past mistakes to avoid future ones, and treating the illness to save the patient" (惩前毖后、治病救人), uniting comrades, and unifying thought. This law has been repeatedly verified in the successive Party rectification campaigns and intra-Party concentrated education activities since the founding of New China. This fully demonstrates that theoretical clarity is not only the prerequisite for the political steadfastness of individual Party members, but also the "purifier" for the entire organization to maintain political unity and consistency of action.

III

Achieving the guarantee of political steadfastness through theoretical clarity requires, above all, taking theoretical cognition as the foundation, persisting in the integration of learning, thinking, and application (学思用贯通) and the unity of knowledge, conviction, and action (知信行统一), and rallying the mighty force for building and strengthening the military through the high degree of unity of thought and will.

Emphasize genuine study to achieve deep knowledge. There is no shortcut to achieving theoretical clarity — it requires genuine and earnest effort in study. For military Party member cadres, theoretical study must be treated as a political responsibility, a spiritual pursuit, and a way of life, studying in depth, studying from the heart, and studying in a grounded manner. One must persist in reading the original works, studying the original texts, and comprehending the original principles, carrying forward the spirit of "squeezing" and "drilling" (挤和钻), settling down to read and re-read, and grasping the theoretical system and core tenets through painstaking effort. One must persist in comprehensive and systematic study, emphasizing the grasp of the systematic and holistic nature of theory, and guarding against seeing only the trees and not the forest. One must always adhere to a problem-oriented approach, studying in connection with the realities of one's work, and being adept at finding ideas, methods, and answers to problems within the Party's innovative theories.

Emphasize self-examination to promote self-renewal. Achieving theoretical clarity must absolutely not stop at mastering some scattered viewpoints and conclusions; one must truly grasp the reasoning, academic principles, and philosophical principles (道理学理哲理) contained therein, and use them to continuously promote self-purification, self-improvement, self-renewal, and self-elevation. One must regularly use the Party's innovative theories to examine and reflect upon one's inner world, consciously applying them to the introspective examination of why one joined the Party, why one became a soldier, and what it means to be a commander (入党干什么、当兵干什么、为将干什么), persisting in transforming thought through study, elevating one's realm through reflection, and strengthening capabilities through application, continuously improving one's ability to fulfill one's duties. One must deeply implement the "two combinations" (两个结合), integrating the study of the basic principles of Marxism with the study of China's excellent traditional military culture, and continuously elevating the wisdom and capabilities for building a strong military and winning battles within a broader grand historical perspective.

Emphasize steadfast action to strengthen the assumption of responsibility. The fundamental criterion for judging whether one is theoretically clear and politically steadfast is whether one can bravely assume responsibility and promote development in practice. Only through repeated tempering in the great crucible of the practice of building a strong nation and a strong military can theoretical understanding be sublimated into firm political conviction and tenacious fighting spirit. At present, the great struggle we are engaged in is long-term, complex, and severe. It requires Party member cadres to dare to withstand trials amid storms, to step forward in the face of urgent, difficult, dangerous, and arduous tasks, and to hold firm in their stance on safeguarding national sovereignty, security, and development interests. Military Party member cadres must persist in using the Party's innovative theories to consolidate their foundations and cultivate their roots (固本培元), establish and practice a correct view of political achievements (政绩观), focus on the hard work of achieving the centenary goal of army-building and high-quality advancement of national defense and military modernization, examine real conditions, produce real measures, and seek real results. In the face of contradictions and difficulties in reform and transformation, risks and challenges in construction and development, and various urgent, difficult, dangerous, and arduous tasks, they must dare to press forward in the face of difficulty and charge at the forefront, striving to write a new chapter in the modernization of national defense and the military.

(Written by: Zhou Junjie)

政治上的坚定，源于理论上的清醒；理论上的成熟，决定政治上的成熟。对于党员干部而言，理论清醒是立身之本、成事之基，是站稳政治立场、把准政治方向、坚守政治信仰、严守政治纪律的根本前提。当前，在纷繁复杂的国内外形势下，面对各种风险挑战与不良思想侵蚀，只有始终保持理论上的高度清醒，筑牢信仰之基、补足精神之钙、把稳思想之舵，才能以坚如磐石的政治定力践行初心使命、勇担时代重任。 一 以理论上的清醒保证政治上的坚定，是立足世情、国情、党情、军情深刻变化，传承建军治军历史经验作出的科学论断，具有深邃历史底蕴与重大现实意义。 当前，中华民族伟大复兴战略全局和世界百年未有之大变局历史性交汇。从国际看，全球动荡变革期特征明显，大国博弈复杂激烈，西方敌对势力对我国全方位遏制、围堵、打压的图谋从未改变，铸魂与蛀魂、固根与毁根的较量一刻也没有停歇。从国内看，我国发展进入战略机遇和风险挑战并存、不确定难预料因素增多时期，人民军队正处于实现建军一百年奋斗目标的关键时期，改革发展稳定任务之重、矛盾风险挑战之多，都是前所未有的。面对严峻形势与矛盾问题，如果缺乏理论清醒，就会缺失“任凭风浪起，稳坐钓鱼船”的政治定力，就很容易在重大原则问题上迷失方向、动摇立场，就很可能在纷繁复杂的矛盾中看不清本质、抓不住要害，最终影响党和人民事业的顺利推进。 回望党的百余年奋斗史，以理论创新引领实践探索贯穿始终。大革命失败后，党从血的教训中深刻认识到必须把马克思主义基本原理同中国具体实际相结合，开辟“农村包围城市、武装夺取政权”的正确道路，推动革命的星火渐成燎原之势。延安整风运动更是一次全党大范围的马克思主义教育运动，通过系统地学理论，清算了主观主义、宗派主义和党八股，实现了全党思想上、政治上、组织上的空前团结统一，为夺取抗日战争和解放战争胜利奠定了坚实的思想政治基础。新时代以来，我们党开展的数次主题教育，无一不是以学理论为先导，用统一思想来凝聚意志、整齐步伐。成功实践昭示，坚持不懈用科学理论武装全党，是我们党不断战胜困难、修正错误、从胜利走向胜利的宝贵经验，是保持党的先进性、纯洁性和战斗力的根本途径。 对于人民军队而言，实行进步的思想贯注，坚持用科学理论武装，是我们这支武装力量的先进性所在。设想，如果我军在理论上不清醒，就好比在战场上失去了指引，战士们往哪儿冲都不知道，又如何能够取胜？作为习近平新时代中国特色社会主义思想的重要组成部分，习近平强军思想是党的军事指导理论最新成果，是坚持走中国特色强军之路、全面推进国防和军队现代化的行动纲领。深入学习贯彻这一思想，对我军来说就是最大的理论清醒。只有学深悟透其核心要义、精神实质、丰富内涵和实践要求，才能深刻理解我们这支人民军队的初心使命、光荣传统和制胜法宝，才能在纷繁复杂的科技之变、战争之变、对手之变中看清本质、把握规律，练就强军胜战的过硬本领。 二 以理论上的清醒保证政治上的坚定，包含丰富哲理，蕴含深刻规律，从不同维度揭示出理论与政治的内在关联。 彰显政治能力提升规律。面对重大问题、处在关键时刻，是旗帜鲜明还是态度暧昧，是立场坚定还是摇摆不定，说到底考验的是政治能力。这种能力养成绝非一日之功，根基在于深厚的理论素养。若缺乏理论思维的深度，就很容易被一些表象所迷惑，从而做出错误判断。只有系统掌握马克思主义立场观点方法，才能透过现象看本质、从政治上看问题，炼就一双“火眼金睛”。革命战争年代的很多俘虏兵，“昨天在白军不勇敢，今天在红军很勇敢”，就生动地说明了理论上真正觉醒了，政治上、情感上才能产生认同，进而才会转变成政治坚定。因此，理论上的清醒往往是提升政治判断力、政治领悟力、政治执行力的内生动力，是能将科学的理论转化为改造世界的强大物质力量。 彰显党性修养规律。理想信念作为共产党人、革命军人的政治灵魂和精神支柱，是我们安身立命的根本。我党我军是用理想信念凝聚起来的，许多革命前辈为什么能够为理想信念而奋斗终身，甚至不惜牺牲生命？就在于他们通过深入的理论学习，坚信自己所追求的事业是正义的、是符合历史发展方向的。毛泽东同志曾说：“主义譬如一面旗子，旗子立起了，大家才有所指望，才知所趋赴。”这个“主义”就是信仰。如果对马克思主义不真学真懂真信真用，那么理想信念就成了无源之水、无本之木，一遇到艰难险阻就可能发生动摇。只有通过系统学习辩证唯物主义和历史唯物主义，掌握了洞察世界的望远镜、显微镜，才能在风浪考验面前站稳脚跟，在诱惑围猎面前稳住心神，在复杂严峻斗争中保持本色，矢志不渝为中国特色社会主义共同理想而奋斗。 彰显整风整训规律。我党我军每逢重要历史关头，总是以整风整训解决党内存在的突出问题，确保队伍团结统一、纯洁光荣。延安整风运动成功的关键，就是把理论学习放在首位。运动之初，党中央明确规定了必读的马列主义经典著作和中央文件，要求全党特别是高级干部下大功夫学习。正是理论上先学一步，大家才有了一把衡量是非的共同“尺子”。随后开展的批评和自我批评，则是以理论为指导、以事实为依据的思想交锋。通过这种方式，大家分清了什么是马克思主义、什么是主观主义，什么是无产阶级思想、什么是非无产阶级思想，从而达到了“惩前毖后、治病救人”、团结同志、统一思想的目的。这一规律在新中国成立后的历次整党整风和党内集中教育活动中，也得到了反复验证。这充分说明，理论清醒不仅是党员个体政治坚定的前提，也是整个组织保持政治统一和行动一致的“净化器”。 三 实现以理论上的清醒保证政治上的坚定，重在以理论认知为根基，坚持学思用贯通、知信行统一，以思想意志的高度统一凝聚起强军兴军的磅礴力量。 注重以真学求深知。实现理论清醒并无捷径，非得下一番真功夫学。对于军队党员干部而言，必须把理论学习作为一种政治责任、一种精神追求、一种生活方式，做到往深里学、往心里学、往实里学。要坚持读原著、学原文、悟原理，发扬“挤”和“钻”的精神，静下心来反复研读，在如琢如磨中把握思想体系与核心要义。要坚持全面系统学，注重把握理论的体系性整体性，防止只见树木不见森林。要始终坚持问题导向，结合工作实际学，善于从党的创新理论中找寻破解问题的思路、方法和答案。 注重以自省促自新。实现理论上的清醒，决不能止步于掌握一些零散的观点和结论，而必须真正掌握蕴含其中的道理学理哲理，并用以不断促进自我净化、自我完善、自我革新、自我提高。要经常性地用党的创新理论来审视和观照个人的内心世界，自觉用于剖析入党干什么、当兵干什么、为将干什么的反躬自省，坚持在学习中改造思想、在思考中提升境界、在运用中增强本领，不断提高履职能力。要深入贯彻“两个结合”，将学习马克思主义基本原理同学习中华优秀传统军事文化贯通起来，在更广阔的大历史视野中不断提升强军胜战的智慧和本领。 注重以笃行强担当。判断理论上是否清醒、政治上是否坚定的根本标准，要看能否在实践中勇担当、促发展。只有在强国强军实践的大熔炉中反复淬炼，理论认识才能升华为坚定的政治信念和顽强的斗争精神。当前，我们正在进行的伟大斗争是长期的、复杂的、严峻的，要求党员干部务必敢于在风浪中经受考验，在急难险重任务面前挺身而出，在维护国家主权、安全、发展利益上立场坚定。军队党员干部必须坚持用党的创新理论固本培元，树立和践行正确政绩观，聚焦攻坚建军一百年奋斗目标和高质量推进国防和军队现代化，察实情、出实招、求实效，面对改革转型中的矛盾难题、建设发展中的风险挑战和各项急难险重任务，敢于迎难而上、冲锋在前，奋力谱写出国防和军队现代化的新篇章。
