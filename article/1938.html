South China Sea Coast Guard Political Work

Party Flag Flying Over the South China Sea: Ships Bear Witness to the Red Legacy

南海之上 党旗飘扬“舰”证红色传承
PLA Daily (解放军报) 30 June 2026
View original source ↗
About this analysis
Summary
CCG vessels Dong'an and Nanxun, operating at Xianbin Reef in the South China Sea, held a themed Party Day activity ahead of the July 1st CCP founding anniversary. This is standard political work content tied to the Party calendar; its value is as a record of how the CCG integrates political education into forward-deployed operations at a contested feature, not as evidence of changed posture or escalated activity at Xianbin Reef.
Translation

At present, the China Coast Guard vessels Dong'an and Nanxun are conducting routine rights-protection and law-enforcement missions in the waters at and around Xianbin Reef in China's South China Sea. In anticipation of July 1st, the Coast Guard vessels jointly held a themed Party Day activity (主题党日活动) amid the vast expanse of the South China Sea.
