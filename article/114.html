About this analysis
Summary
A PLA Daily report describes concurrent training activities by Tibet Military District and Xinjiang Military District brigades, Rocket Force and Air Force units, and People's Armed Police Tibet General Corps elements on high-altitude plateau training grounds, emphasizing integration of UAVs, data links, robotic systems, and simulation technology under the rubric of 'new-quality combat capabilities.' The report signals a deliberate PLA effort to accelerate multi-domain, intelligentized training specifically adapted to high-altitude conditions along the Indian border, with the Gangbala Radar Station's shift to a remote duty mode and the normalization of joint exercises between ground and air units indicating incremental but concrete improvements in readiness and interoperability in the Western Theater. The piece is useful as evidence that the PLA is treating the plateau not as a logistical constraint to be managed but as a training environment to be exploited for systems-integration exercises.
Translation

Reforming Training Concepts, Upgrading Training Methods, Reshaping the Training Landscape: New-Quality Combat Capabilities (新质战斗力) Accelerating Generation on the High-Altitude Front Line. PLA Daily report by correspondents Fan Enda and special correspondent Qi Xucong: New-type artillery tempers its edge on the plateau, unmanned aerial vehicles patrol the skies, data links connect across all domains, and fire units precisely lock onto the "enemy"… Not long ago, on a plateau training ground, a certain brigade of the Tibet Military District focused on generating new-quality combat capabilities and conducted various training activities in an orderly manner.

New forces are accelerating their entry into the order of battle, new equipment is being fielded in a continuous stream, new tactics and methods of warfare are steadily taking hold… In recent days, reporters visited multiple exercise and training sites on the high-altitude front line and observed that units are focusing on actual combat requirements, continuously reforming training concepts, upgrading training methods, and reshaping the training landscape. A vivid scene of intensive combat readiness training has unfolded, with new-quality combat capabilities accelerating their accumulation and generation on the high-altitude front line.

From "waiting for orders" to "seizing the moment," officers and soldiers of the 1st Battalion of a certain Rocket Force brigade have abandoned traditional training methods, conducting more than ten realistic combat-oriented training subjects in a continuous track-style sequence, putting into practice the spirit of high-altitude Rocket Force soldiers through the conscious pursuit of "one second faster in speed, one point greater in the odds of victory." From "passive adaptation" to "active adaptation," a certain Air Force unit, in combination with the special high-altitude environment, has made targeted modifications to key systems of its combat aircraft, precisely matching high-altitude characteristics and actual mission requirements, and has concentrated on drilling subjects such as extreme maneuvering, low-altitude penetration, and ground attack, maximizing the excavation of equipment's combat potential.

Upgrading training methods, technology empowerment activates a "new engine" of combat power. Inside simulation training cabins, officers and soldiers of a certain regiment of the Xinjiang Military District "drive" equipment vehicles over high-altitude mountain passes (达坂), with weather conditions, time periods, and road conditions switching randomly, forging their skills to fight and win through immersive training. On the training ground of a certain detachment of the People's Armed Police Tibet General Corps, infrared unmanned aerial vehicles conduct overhead reconnaissance and precise positioning, intelligent robot dogs charge forward in assault, and unmanned equipment is deeply coupled with individual soldier combat, building a solid and efficient security barrier through intelligentized (智能化) training… Each unit is exploring pathways to generate new-quality combat capabilities, continuously advancing the new "intelligent+" (智能+) training model, and driving high-altitude training toward a leap to intelligent and precision training.

At a certain position, the Gangbala Radar Station of a certain Air Force unit has used technological means to achieve a remote off-site duty mode, with officers and soldiers' combat positions moved down more than one thousand meters in altitude. No longer distracted by coping with the harsh environment, they are able to invest more energy in combat readiness duty and combat power enhancement, routinely conducting networked adversarial exercises and live-equipment training, with the unit's rapid mobility capability and early warning capability markedly improved.

Reshaping the training landscape, systems-based (体系) joint action concentrates "new momentum" for victory. A certain Air Force unit has proactively integrated into the system, engaging in adversarial exercises against fighter aviation units that have come to conduct temporary training, sparring against various types of unmanned aerial vehicles, continuously forging hard anti-jamming skills in electromagnetic offense and defense, and steadily enhancing actual combat capability through repeated realistic combat training. A certain brigade of the Xinjiang Military District has joined with neighboring Air Force units to routinely conduct joint exercises and training, opening up command links and sharing air situation awareness, achieving full-element joint action and integrated resistance in fire strikes… Each unit has abandoned the concept and practice of "fighting independently and going it alone," proactively breaking down barriers between services and branches and clearing coordination bottlenecks, driving the overall quality improvement and comprehensive effectiveness enhancement of new-quality combat capability generation.

Once, this snow-covered plateau was a "forbidden zone for life" that inspired dread; today, it has become a "forge of soldiers" (砺兵高地) that drives units' combat readiness training to go deeper and become more substantive. Through exercise after exercise and charge after charge, units stationed on the plateau have set their sights on the laws governing the generation of new-quality combat capabilities, continuously breaking free from fixed mindsets, and making solid efforts toward what is new and strong, exploring methods and pathways to liberate and develop new-quality combat capabilities. "Although it is bitterly cold, oxygen-deficient, and environmentally harsh here, our confidence in winning battles is growing stronger and stronger!" said one high-altitude soldier.

革新训练理念 升级训练手段 重塑训练格局 新质战斗力在高原一线加速生成 解放军报讯 记者范恩达、特约记者齐旭聪报道：新型火炮砺剑高原，无人机巡弋长空，数据链路全域贯通，火力单元精准锁“敌”……前不久，高原练兵场上，西藏军区某旅聚焦新质战斗力生成，有序开展各项训练。 新力量加速入列，新装备不断列装，新战法持续落地……连日来，记者踏访高原一线多个演训场看到，各部队聚焦实战需求，持续革新训练理念、升级训练手段、重塑训练格局，一幅火热的练兵备战图景铺展开来，新质战斗力在高原一线加速集聚生成。 从“等指令”到“抢时机”，火箭军某旅一营官兵摒弃传统训法，以赛道式连贯展开10余个实战化课目训练，用“速度快一秒、胜算多一分”的自觉追求践行高原火箭兵精神；从“被动适应”到“主动适配”，空军某部结合高原特殊环境，针对性改装战机关键系统，精准匹配高原特点和任务实际，专攻精练极限机动、低空突防、对地打击等课目，最大限度挖掘装备作战潜能。 升级训练手段，科技赋能激活战力“新引擎”。模拟训练舱内，新疆军区某团官兵“驾驶”装备车辆翻越高原达坂，天候、时段、路况随机切换，沉浸式锤炼打赢本领；武警西藏总队某支队训练场上，红外无人机临空侦察、精准定位，智能机器狗冲锋突击，无人装备与单兵作战深度耦合，以智能化练兵构筑起坚固高效的安全屏障……各部队探索新质战斗力生成路径，持续推开“智能+”练兵新模式，推动高原训练向智能精训跨越。 某点位上，空军某部甘巴拉雷达站运用科技手段实现异地远程遥控值勤模式，官兵战位下沉千余米。不再为对抗恶劣环境分心，他们把更多精力投入战备值勤和战斗力提升上，常态开展联网对抗、实装实训，部队快速机动能力和预警能力明显提升。 重塑训练格局，体系联动凝聚胜战“新动能”。空军某部主动融入体系，与前来驻训的歼击机部队互为对手展开对抗，与各型无人机过招较量，在电磁攻防中不断锤炼反干扰硬功，在一次次实战化训练中持续提升实战能力；新疆军区某旅携手空军友邻部队常态开展联演联训，打通指挥链路、共享空情态势，实现火力打击全要素联动、一体化抗击……各部队摒弃“各自为战、单打独斗”的观念做法，主动打破军兵种壁垒、打通协同堵点，推动新质战斗力生成整体提质、全面增效。 曾经，这片雪域高原是令人望而生畏的“生命禁区”；如今，这里已成为牵引部队练兵备战走深走实的“砺兵高地”。在一场场演训、一次次冲锋中，驻高原各部队瞄准新质战斗力生成规律，不断打破思维定式，向新向强扎实用力，探寻解放和发展新质战斗力的方法路径。“这里虽然高寒缺氧、环境艰苦，但我们的胜战底气越来越足！”一名高原战士说。
