[Bilingual] Chinese Military Faithfully Fulfills Its Peacekeeping Missions to Contribute More of China's Strength to Safeguarding World Peace

【双语】中国军队忠实履行维和使命 为维护世界和平贡献更多中国力量
PLA Daily (解放军报) 18 May 2026
View original source ↗
About this analysis
Summary
At a May 18 MND press conference, spokesperson Senior Colonel Jiang Bin confirmed that China's third-rotation Quick Reaction Force has deployed to Abyei to relieve the second rotation, with the outgoing unit having received a UN commendation letter and Peace Honor Medals from UNISFA for its performance on armed patrols, VIP protection, and emergency response. The article is a routine rotation announcement; its narrow use is as a baseline record of the QRF rotation cycle and the institutional framing Beijing applies to peacekeeping contributions — emphasizing professionalism and UN recognition — rather than evidence of any change in force posture or mission scope in Abyei.
Translation

Chinese Military Faithfully Fulfills Its Peacekeeping Missions to Contribute More of China's Strength to Safeguarding World Peace

On the afternoon of May 18, Ministry of National Defense spokesperson Senior Colonel Jiang Bin released information on recent military-related issues.

Reporter: According to reports, China's third rotation Quick Reaction Force (CHN QRF) to Abyei recently departed for the mission area to relieve the second rotation and carry out peacekeeping duties. Could you provide further details?

Jiang Bin: Amid a severe security situation and complex natural environment, the Chinese peacekeeping Quick Reaction Force deployed to Abyei has successfully completed tasks including armed patrols, VIP protection, and rapid response to emergencies. The unit has also actively carried out military-civilian co-construction (军民共建) and cultural exchange activities, earning broad trust from the local population. The United Nations Interim Security Force for Abyei sent a letter of commendation expressing gratitude for the high level of professionalism and dedication demonstrated by the Chinese peacekeeping Quick Reaction Force during its mission period, and awarded United Nations Peace Honor Medals to the officers and soldiers of the unit.

Upholding peace, pressing forward with courage and resolve. Through concrete actions, the Chinese military has demonstrated that peace is not a slogan, but a commitment and steadfastness willing to confront danger head-on. We will actively honor our solemn commitment, faithfully fulfill our peacekeeping missions, and contribute more of China's strength to safeguarding world peace.
