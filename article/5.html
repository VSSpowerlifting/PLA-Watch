Grasping the Key Points for Accelerating the Development of National Defense Mobilization Capabilities

■ Xia Wenxiang, Su Cao

Introduction

National defense mobilization, as the bridge and link between national defense construction and economic and social development, is an important support for accumulating national defense potential, enhancing national defense strength, and winning future wars. At present, the international strategic landscape is undergoing profound transformation, the risks and challenges facing national security are growing increasingly complex, and the military revolution and the scientific and technological revolution are intertwining and compounding—all of which have placed new and higher demands on national defense mobilization. We must keep pace with the times, align closely with the requirements of war, and stay grounded in national conditions, so as to identify the key points for accelerating national defense mobilization capability development and provide support for winning modern wars and shaping a lasting advantage in great-power competition.

The Foundation Lies in Capability—Concentrating Efforts on Building a Multi-Dimensional, Composite Mobilization System Capability

The core objective of accelerating national defense mobilization capability development is system capability. The continuously accelerating tempo of modern warfare and the rapid evolution of operational patterns demand that national defense mobilization transition from single-domain capability to system capability. The U.S.-Israel-Iran conflict has demonstrated that national defense mobilization system capability has already become the focal point of competition among the parties to a war.

Grasping the connotation of national defense mobilization system capability. National defense mobilization involves broad-area coverage, demand-driven conversion, and concentrated application of advantages; its capability is not the traditional physical assembly of resources, but rather a system capability encompassing storage, dispatch, conversion, transportation, and employment. First is agile response capability: the ability to perceive the situation in real time, accurately assess requirements, rapidly decompose tasks, and immediately trigger mobilization mechanisms. Second is rapid conversion capability: relying on standardized contingency plans and flat command structures, the ability to rapidly convert potential in personnel, finance, materiel, technology, and intelligence into actual capabilities supporting combat or emergency tasks. Third is efficient integration capability: the ability to comprehensively integrate full-domain, multi-dimensional, and fragmented resources through integration, reorganization, and embedding, so as to build organizational power, supply power, support power, and combat power that reach directly to the battlefield. Fourth is dynamic balancing capability: the ability to rationally regulate the relationships between wartime support and peacetime construction, resource input and effectiveness output, core requirements and diverse adaptability, and emergency support and sustained endurance, achieving a reasonable balance and coordination between supporting combat operations and maintaining the functioning of society.

Strengthening the orientation of national defense mobilization system capability. The core objective of accelerating national defense mobilization capability development is to accelerate the formation and conversion of war sustainment capability. We must adhere to orienting toward and for war, and leading construction through war, aligning the orientation of national defense mobilization capability with the orientation of combat capability, and fully embedding the combat effectiveness standard into national defense mobilization planning and construction. We must conduct in-depth research into the mechanisms for achieving victory in informatized and intelligentized warfare, analyze future war mobilization requirements, and—grounded in extreme conditions, emphasizing cutting-edge advantages, and connecting to war preparation and fighting—ensure that the "supply side" of mobilization capability always closely tracks the "demand side" of winning and achieving victory. We must rectify the functional center of gravity of national defense mobilization in converting potential into reality and supporting victory in war, place the foundation of national defense mobilization preparation on supporting victory in the people's war of the intelligent era, adhere to "everything for the front, everything for victory," always focus on wartime, orient toward the battlefield, and cultivate combat power; stockpile resources, strengthen forces, improve mechanisms, and rigorously conduct exercises and training, so as to expeditiously complete construction of a new-era national defense mobilization system commensurate with military strategy and capability requirements.

Establishing the benchmark for national defense mobilization system capability. National defense mobilization bears the functional mission of deterring war, participating in war, supporting war, and sustaining war. We must take forging system capability commensurate with modern warfare as the core benchmark of national defense mobilization, running it through every link and the entire process of national defense mobilization. Adhere to simultaneous struggle, war preparation, and construction; solidly carry out preparatory work for mobilization to lead off, be applied throughout, and be adjusted as needed; refine and improve the rapid peacetime-to-wartime transition mechanism; and always maintain a good state of full-time readiness and ability to fight at any time. Improve the system for testing and evaluating mobilization capability, using war design as the source, mobilization actions as the main thread, and system capability as the benchmark, to comprehensively evaluate national defense mobilization capabilities across all domains step by step, guiding iterative optimization of national defense mobilization capability. Adhere to promoting construction through use, and leverage major disaster relief operations, military-civilian joint exercises and training, and special mission practices to survey the status of potential resources, examine the validity of case verification and case development, check the connectivity of command chains, test the combined employment of forces, and analyze the effectiveness of mobilization implementation, so as to comprehensively test and improve the level of national defense mobilization in war preparation and construction.

The Key Lies in Construction—Jointly Building a Military-Civilian Integrated Capability Emergence Mechanism

Accelerating national defense mobilization capability development is an arduous strategic task and a complex systems engineering project; grasping the connotation, essential meaning, and methodological principles of construction is of critical importance. We should focus on the complexity of national defense mobilization, grasp the requirements of the era with strategic vision, break through development difficulties with innovative thinking, consolidate the foundation of construction with pragmatic measures, and drive comprehensive upgrading through key breakthroughs, so as to progressively build a military-civilian integrated national defense mobilization capability emergence mechanism.

Adhere to demand-driven construction, activating the source of capability emergence. Demand-driven planning and planning-led resource allocation are the basic laws of strategic management and the basic basis of national defense mobilization. The current main bottlenecks and difficulties are unclear channels for transmitting requirements and undefined methods for converting them; the key to resolution is to establish a military requirements decoding system, with accurate submission, scientific conversion, rapid confirmation, and efficient implementation as the main thread, effectively converting requirements from the military domain into technical indicators and task requirements for national scientific research, production, and construction. We must break down the professional barriers between the military and civilian sectors, open up secure channels for transmitting national defense mobilization requirements information, ensure that requirements are accurately translated, completely transmitted, and thoroughly understood and implemented, and truly put into practice. Emphasize focusing on capability "gaps": both give full play to the military's leading role in researching, demonstrating, and putting forward requirements, and give full play to the local sector's active role in anticipating tasks, matching military requirements, and driving capability improvement, so as to bidirectionally calibrate the target orientation of national defense mobilization capability development and activate the inherent potential of the supply side.

Emphasize planning-led construction, seizing the key to capability emergence. The scientific formulation and successive implementation of five-year plans is an important experience of our Party in governing the country and a major institutional arrangement for advancing the modernization of national defense and the armed forces. Accelerating national defense mobilization capability development must be coordinated and advanced within the larger strategic framework of Chinese-style modernization (中国式现代化), incorporated into the national security system for sound top-level design, integrated into the process of high-quality advancement of national defense and military modernization for synchronized deployment and implementation, and the 15th Five-Year Plan for national defense mobilization construction and related special plans must be scientifically compiled and implemented. We must focus on improving the contribution rate to serving and supporting victory in high-end warfare, emphasize asymmetric counterbalancing, adhere to planning-led resource allocation, prioritize resolving critical capability shortfalls, emphasize improving scarce and urgently needed capabilities, and leverage overall capability to advance comprehensively to a higher level.

Strengthen military-civilian integration, opening up channels for capability emergence. National defense mobilization connects the demand and supply ends, involves both the military and civilian lines, and runs through both the economic and defense domains; accelerating its capability generation must emphasize military-civilian linkage, coordinated planning, and system-level effort. We must adhere to military-civilian coordination, strengthen the "one board" (一盘棋) thinking among military and civilian organizations at all levels, advance the alignment of mobilization concepts between military and civilian, the joint establishment of command organizations, the unified integration of plans and schemes, and the fusion of force reliance, so as to efficiently advance national defense mobilization capability improvement. Emphasize consistency between construction and use: based on typical task scenarios of national defense mobilization participating in, supporting, and sustaining war, connect the construction management chain with the command and employment chain; both accurately grasp "what is available, how much there is, and where it is," and thoroughly analyze "what to use, how much to use, and how to use it," so as to promote coupling between combat and mobilization and consistency between construction and use.

The Essential Point Is Acceleration—Rapidly Forming a Capability Leap Path That Is Fast, Good, and Economical

In all martial arts under heaven, only speed is unbreakable (天下武功，唯快不破). Sufficient speed can often compensate for systemic weakness. Recent international conflicts such as the U.S.-Israel-Iran conflict have demonstrated that the trends toward linkage, suddenness, and variability in modern warfare have placed increasingly urgent demands on the efficiency of generating and releasing national defense mobilization capabilities, objectively requiring an acceleration of the speed of national defense mobilization capability generation—the two characters "accelerate" (加快) highlight the essential meaning of the times for national defense mobilization capability development.

Strengthen strategic coordination to plan for speed, leading capability leaps in planning and design. Fully leverage the system advantages, institutional advantages, and scale advantages of the national defense mobilization system; seize the opportunity of China's vigorous development of new-quality productive forces (新质生产力); and put in sufficient effort and make full use of the space for speed in coordinated planning. As an important component of the national strategic system, national defense mobilization has the distinctive characteristics of military-civilian integration, peacetime-wartime integration, and front-rear integration; through efficient strategic coordination, it must both effectively enhance the state's capacity to respond to war and emergencies and empower high-quality economic development, so as to fully leverage the advantages of the whole-nation system (举国体制). In particular, as an important carrier for the efficient fusion and bidirectional driving of new-quality productive forces and new-quality combat capabilities (新质战斗力), national defense mobilization must possess efficient coordination capability in order to aggregate industrial advantages, scientific and technological advantages, talent advantages, and organizational advantages into strategic system advantages, laying a solid foundation for consolidating and improving the integrated national strategic system and capabilities.

Deepen management innovation to seek speed, driving capability leaps through institutional mechanisms. Management is also combat effectiveness. We must innovate the strategic management operational chain of national defense mobilization, improve the agile response and rapid conversion mechanism for advanced technologies, abandon traditional path dependencies, and accelerate efforts in technology empowerment, mechanism innovation, and system reconstruction, so as to shorten the national defense mobilization capability generation cycle. Strengthen cross-military-civilian governance, break down the barriers of the military-civilian dual structure, differences in thinking and concepts, and the restrictions of vertical-horizontal segmentation, and through process loading, functional fusion, and digital-intelligent support, build a cross-military-civilian working framework in which each fulfills its responsibilities, cooperates closely, and operates in a standardized and orderly manner. Integrate military and civilian procurement standards, simplify mobilization procurement links, optimize the redundancy design of pre-positioned and pre-fabricated stocks, and through management innovation and institutional innovation compress the cycle for converting economic strength into combat capability, maintaining and enhancing the resilience of industrial and supply chains.

Emphasize science and technology empowerment to promote speed, supporting capability leaps in methods and means. Science and technology have improved the efficiency of human production and labor and the efficiency of human perception and understanding of the world; the integrated development of mechanization, informatization, and intelligentization is systematically and comprehensively empowering the process of war and war preparation. We must emphasize updating mobilization concepts, innovating mobilization models, and reforming mobilization actions; emphasize the construction of strategic science and technology forces, the research, development, and production of cutting-edge weapons and equipment, and the optimized organization of new-quality force teams; and rely on science and technology empowerment to lead the accelerated "metamorphosis" (蝶变) of national defense mobilization capabilities and drive the overall transformation of national defense mobilization. Accelerate the supply and stockpiling of unmanned intelligent combat and counter capabilities, lower access thresholds, expand participation pathways, enrich scenario applications, and effectively open up the mobilization conversion chain for the effective aggregation and release of modern science and technology, continuously cultivating and expanding new-domain, new-quality mobilization growth poles (新域新质动员增长极), and driving national defense mobilization capabilities to improve rapidly, innovatively, and with enhanced effectiveness.
