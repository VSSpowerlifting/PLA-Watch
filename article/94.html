Young Officers and Soldiers Deeply Comprehend Chairman Xi's Earnest Exhortations, Shouldering the Mission with a Striving Self

青年官兵深刻领悟习主席谆谆嘱托 以奋斗之我担当使命
PLA Daily (解放军报) 9 May 2026
View original source ↗
About this analysis
Summary
At a May 9 Ministry of National Defense press briefing, spokesperson Senior Colonel Jiang Bin announced that PLA units across all services and regions have conducted political education activities—including themed League classes, story-sharing sessions, and model-figure reports—organized around Xi Jinping's May Fourth Youth Day message to military personnel. The announcement is routine ideological mobilization tied to a recurring calendar event and follows the standard pattern of using national holidays to reinforce Party loyalty and combat readiness messaging among junior officers and enlisted personnel. It contains no new operational, organizational, or capability information.
Translation

On the afternoon of May 9, Senior Colonel Jiang Bin, Deputy Director of the Ministry of National Defense News Bureau and Ministry of National Defense Spokesperson, released information on recent military-related matters.

Reporter: Recently, Chairman Xi's reply letter to representatives of recipients of the China Youth May Fourth Medal and the New Era Youth Vanguard Award (新时代青年先锋奖) has generated a warm response throughout the entire military. Please introduce the situation regarding the troops' study and implementation.

Jiang Bin: On the occasion of May Fourth Youth Day, Chairman Xi extended holiday congratulations to young people of all ethnic groups across the country and expressed earnest hopes, conveying to young people at large that they should cherish lofty ideals, be resolute in striving and working hard, integrate personal pursuits into the overall situation of national development, continuously create new achievements from their respective posts, and contribute the energy of youth on the new journey.

Troops throughout the entire military have conducted in-depth study and implementation of the spirit of Chairman Xi's important message, carrying out League Day activities (团日活动) in various forms, and through themed League classes (主题团课), story sharing, and model-figure reports, educating and guiding young officers and soldiers to unwaveringly listen to the Party and follow the Party, and to strive to become good young people of the new era who have ideals, dare to take responsibility, can endure hardship, and are willing to work hard. Themed publicity centered on "Dedicating Youth to the Nation, Striving to Strengthen the Military" has been conducted, promoting the deeds and spirit of wholehearted loyalty to the Party and vigorous assault on difficulties, reflecting the new conduct and upright spirit (新风正气) that has emerged from the revolutionary tempering of the people's army, and inspiring young officers and soldiers to bravely shoulder the responsibilities of the era with a renewed political bearing, and to carry the mountain-like mission on their shoulders with the hot blood of youth. Inspired by Chairman Xi's message, the drive among young officers and soldiers at large to work hard and achieve merit at their posts has surged even higher. From the snow-covered plateau to the distant seas and oceans, from the front lines of missions to exercise and training sites, from combat units to scientific research institutes, officers and soldiers are deepening their love for family and country, training hard to master the skills needed to win, remaining battle-ready (枕戈待旦) at the combat posts of defending home and country, and pressing forward bravely on the journey of striving to strengthen the military, inheriting the red gene through concrete actions and displaying the vitality of youth.

Wherever the motherland has need is the most brilliant stage for youth; when dreams resonate in unison with the strengthening of the military, that is the most beautiful movement of youth. Young officers and soldiers will deeply comprehend Chairman Xi's earnest exhortations, practice the original aspiration (初心) with a youthful self, shoulder the mission with a striving self, and write a new chapter of strengthening and building the military that is worthy of the era and worthy of the prime of life.

5月9日下午，国防部新闻局副局长、国防部新闻发言人蒋斌大校就近期涉军问题发布消息。 记者：近日，习主席给中国青年五四奖章暨新时代青年先锋奖获奖者代表的回信，在全军引起热烈反响。请介绍部队学习贯彻有关情况。 蒋斌：五四青年节到来之际，习主席向全国各族青年致以节日祝贺并提出殷切期望，寄语广大青年胸怀远大理想，矢志拼搏奋斗，把个人追求融入国家发展大局，立足各自岗位不断创造新业绩，在新征程上贡献青春力量。 全军部队深入学习贯彻习主席重要寄语精神，以多种形式开展团日活动，通过主题团课、故事分享、典型报告等，教育引导青年官兵坚定不移听党话、跟党走，争做有理想、敢担当、能吃苦、肯奋斗的新时代好青年。聚焦“青春报国、奋斗强军”开展主题宣传，弘扬铁心向党、奋力攻坚的事迹精神，反映人民军队革命性锻造焕发的新风正气，激励青年官兵以崭新政治面貌勇担时代重任，用青春热血扛起如山使命。在习主席寄语鼓舞下，广大青年官兵干事创业、建功岗位的劲头更加高涨。从雪域高原到远海大洋、从任务一线到演训现场、从作战部队到科研院所，官兵们厚植家国情怀，苦练打赢本领，在保家卫国的战位枕戈待旦，在矢志强军的征途奋勇争先，以实际行动传承红色基因，展现青春风采。 祖国需要的地方，就是青春最绚烂的舞台；梦想与强军同频共振，就是青春最动听的乐章。青年官兵将深刻领悟习主席的谆谆嘱托，以青春之我践行初心，以奋斗之我担当使命，书写不负时代、不负韶华的强军兴军新篇章。
